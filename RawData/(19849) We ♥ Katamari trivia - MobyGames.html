Trivia

1001 Video Games

We Love Katamari appears in the book 1001 Video Games You Must Play Before You Die by General Editor Tony Mott.

Bonus video

One of the two unlockable bonus videos in the Katamari Memorial for the PAL release is the introduction cut-scene of Katamari Damacy. The original game was not released in PAL territories.

Description official description

Similar to the Dance Dance Revolution Games, Tap a Jam is an arcade music game where you use a combination of four keys to play a song as the instructions fly by horizontally, at an increasing difficulty. The music list includes over thirty classic tunes with a twist, divided over three difficulty levels. Highscores are kept for each level and you can create your own tunes with the in-game Beat Box.

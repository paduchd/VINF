Description

You are a fighter pilot going through wave after wave of enemies from a 1st Person Perspective. You control a gun-sight and you try to shoot as many planes and ground defences while you fly through the air. Your sights will automatically Lock-On to a target and you can fire a missile to hit them.

Trivia

Cancelled C64 version

This game is unusual among Data East coin-ops of the time in not being converted to a full range of the leading computers. A Commodore 64 version is known to have been in development at Activision, but there is no information about Amiga, Spectrum or others being in development.

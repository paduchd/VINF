Description

NHL All-Star Hockey is the first of Sega's own line of Saturn NHL licensed titles. It includes the ability to play pre-season matches, a full season or just the playoffs, as well as multimedia content, such as a tour of the NHL Hall of Fame in Toronto, information on the trophies and real player trading cards (from the Fleer Ultra collection). It's also possible to trade players and create players and teams for use in pre-season matches.

Trivia

Team Canada

The core game was developed by only five people: Spencer Craske (programmer), Paul Martin (programmer), Dave Forshaw (programmer), Colin Reed (programmer) and Michael A. Upton (artist). They called themselves "Team Canada" and worked for more than a year on the game. Other people were involved as well, such as art director Dave Duncan.

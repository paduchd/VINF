  • Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six
  • Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six: Mission Pack - Eagle Watch

  • Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six: Rogue Spear Mission Pack - Urban Operations

  • Covert Ops Intel - interviews with Tom Clancy, real-life covert operatives and FBI instructors

Trivia

Rainbow Six: Collector's Edition has one of the highest MSRP (Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price) in contemporary game history: $99.95. Most stores sell it for about $70, twice as expensive as the most contemporary bundles or single titles.

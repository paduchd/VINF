Trivia

Hidden Teams

In the game are hidden files of 2 additional teams, Larrousse and Lotus (hidden because they had retired from the F1 in April 1995). Various sponsors and parts are also hidden, for example Marlboro and Ascon as sponsors. You can update and edit the data with a DBF manager if you wish.

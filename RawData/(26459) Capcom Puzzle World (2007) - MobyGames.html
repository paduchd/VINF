Description official description

Capcom Puzzle World is a compilation of Capcom's retro arcade puzzle hits. There are some bonus features to unlock and play with. The games included are:* Block Block

  • Buster Bros.
  • Buster Buddies
  • Super Buster Bros.
  • Super Puzzle Fighter II Turbo (with bonus features based on two Japanese-only versions of the game)

Some games have multiplayer support, with both head-to-head and cooperative 2-player gameplay, and the ability to take screenshots. Block Block can be played with several aspect ratios including normal, stretch and the original vertical orientation.

Trivia

Firmware

In July 2007 the game was released in several European countries, despite of known problems due to the PSP's 3.5 firmware update. For instance, the game is known to lock while playing Super Puzzle Fighter II Turbo. Capcom blames Sony and claims the game worked flawlessly with the 3.4 firmware release. The company is working with Sony to solve the problem, and it will likely be fixed with the next firmware update.

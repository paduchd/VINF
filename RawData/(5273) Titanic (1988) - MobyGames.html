Description

After developing a new suit for diving in deep waters, Urine and Deepman decide to use it to search for the Titanic. This dive will be useful to test the new suit, but also to find the secrets that are hidden in the lost ship.

Fight against all kind of sea lifeforms to make your way to the Titanic. Find the secret that was lost with the ship.

Trivia

PC Version

The PC version of this game was released three years later and doesn't follow the outline of the 8 bit versions verbatim, being more of a "Deluxe" edition. It has two extra stages, as well as VGA graphics and AdLib sounds. PC versions of Spanish games of the era often relied on CGA / EGA graphics and PC Speaker sounds instead.

Description official descriptions

This is third Midway Arcade Treasures compilation released in as many years. This collection focuses on Midway and Atari racing games from the 1990s. The games in this collection include:

  • San Francisco Rush: The Rock Alcatraz Edition
  • Hydro Thunder
  • Super Off Road
  • Badlands
  • Race Drivin'
  • S.T.U.N. Runner
  • San Francisco Rush 2049
  • Offroad Thunder

Some of the games in this collection are based on the home conversions, rather than emulation of the arcade originals.

Trivia

Development

The conversions of Hydro Thunder, Off Road Thunder, and San Francisco Rush were handled by China-based developer Gamestar. San Francisco Rush 2049 and Hydro Thunder are based on the Dreamcast versions, rather than the arcade originals. The other games in this collection are run in emulators written by Digital Eclipse.

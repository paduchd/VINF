Description

This edition of El Matador was exclusively available at two German Saturn branches in Leipzig for the Games Convention 2006. It was a limited production of 2000 units and is the only available uncut version in German. The official release version (which was pushed back) was significantly altered to get an USK 18 rating instead of none.

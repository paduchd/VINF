Description

The compilation Game-Hits 1 offers 10 games from many different genres, spread over 10 CDs. Manuals are only available on CD in PDF format.

The following games are included:

  • Civilization: Call to Power
  • Flying Saucer
  • GP 500
  • King's Quest: Mask of Eternity
  • M1 Tank Platoon 2
  • Max Montezuma: Der Fluch der Azteken
  • Mayday: Conflict Earth
  • MechWarrior 3
  • Spirit of Speed 1937
  • X-COM: Interceptor

Trivia

Errors

  • The game box, the manual, and even the printed side on the X-COM CD is telling, that the game is X-COM: Apocalypse, but in reality, it is X-COM: Interceptor.
  • Do you need a manual for Spirit of Speed 1937? Don't search for it inside the manual directory on that CD, there is only another GP 500 manual...
  • While the year at the end of the Spirit of Speed game title is 1937 in reality, both the back of box and the printed side of the CD are referring to 1939.

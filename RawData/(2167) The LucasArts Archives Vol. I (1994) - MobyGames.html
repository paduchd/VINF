Description official description

This compilation bundles three LucasArts games, along with a couple of extras. The full games are:

  • Indiana Jones and the Fate of Atlantis
  • Maniac Mansion: Day of the Tentacle
  • Sam & Max: Hit the Road

The box also includes demos of Full Throttle, Star Wars: Dark Forces (not present in Mac version) and three playable levels of Star Wars: Rebel Assault, along with a Star Wars Screen Entertainment and a LucasArts SuperSampler disc

Description official description

This expansion pack for The Sims 2 creates a new "Young Adult" age range and sends them off to college. Adding over 125 new items for your dorm, frat house, sorority or favorite hangout, University allows your Sim to experience life on campus. Whether partying or cramming, exploring new careers or trying not to flunk out, playing in the arcade or with the hottest college band, the University and your Sims will never be the same.

Requires The Sims 2, The Sims 2 (Special DVD Edition) or The Sims 2 (Holiday Edition) to play.

Trivia

References

Apparently there are some South Park fans in Maxis' sound department; when a male teen or young adult uses the pool table and misses a shot, one of the gibberish words they might say is "Meekrab." This was a fake swearword used in the infamous "162 Sh*ts" episode of South Park.

Soundtrack

All of the songs on the "College Rock" radio station are actually licensed songs rerecorded by the original artists in Simlish.

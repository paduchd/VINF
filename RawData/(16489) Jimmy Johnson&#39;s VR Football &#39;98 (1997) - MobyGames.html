Trivia

Charity

For every copy of Jimmy Johnson's VR Football '98 purchased, VR Sports promised to donate $1 to the United Way (one of the NFL's favorite charities). They did stipulate a $25,000 maximum limit on their contribution but it is unknown if they actually reached that number of sales.

Description official descriptions

After twelve hours of waiting in the emergency room, a woman dies. The city is rioting over the hospital's shabby work. The player is a doctor in the ER of this hospital. The player must treat many patients, from gunshot wounds to stabbings, while keeping their sanity. Utilizing live-action video, various actor's will keep the story going, while the Chief Of Staff reviews the player's performance.

Description

The Ultimate Trivia Game CD is a trivia game for one to two players. During a round, players select one of three randomly chosen categories and have thirty seconds to select the correct multiple choice answer. While most questions are text-based, the game also has audio and video clips.

The single player game lasts for 100 questions and the two player game lasts for 50. At the game's end, the player's scores are shown as well as a break down of the score into each of the game's ten categories.

Description official description

World Hockey 95 is an ice hockey game focusing on international matches. The player can choose to control any of the leading national ice hockey teams in the world, and compete against other countries. The game has three play modes: practice, exhibition, and tournament. The player can train his team and swap players to make it more efficient in the upcoming tournament. The matches themselves are viewed from an isometric perspective.

Description official description

War Times European Frontline is a 3d Real Time Strategy game (RTS) with resources which takes place in World War II. You can control 4 different nations: Germany, Great Britain, United States and Soviet Union across 32 missions in 2 different campaigns: Axis and Allies.

Trivia

On February 14th, 2005, North American publisher Strategy First lost the publishing rights to the game. According to a developer's press release, the publisher continued to sell and distribute the game after the termination of the agreement and the completion of the sell-of period of the product. Developer Legend Studios was working on a patch with new features, but due to the lack of payment, those new features were never released as a patch for the North American version of the game, but instead appeared in the European version.

In March, 2005, Strategy First released the compilation 6+1 Combat pack, which, according to the developer, illegally includes War Times, an outdated version of the game with no tech, faked cd keys or Internet gaming support.

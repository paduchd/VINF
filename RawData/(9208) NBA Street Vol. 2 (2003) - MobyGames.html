Description official descriptions

Return to the street for the unqiue basketball experience that allows you to show off to the crowd, wear crazy gear, and pull of crazy shots, passes and moves.

Modes include Street School (a tutorial that allows you to learn all the basic and more complex moves of the game), Pick Up Game (exhibition mode, in which you can win points to unlock rewards), NBA Challenge has you competing against the current crop of talent from the NBA, as well as collection of legendary players from the past, and Be a Legend give you the opportunity to create your own original basketball player and take him on a tour to compete against the world's best, unlocking new moves, clothes and other surprises.

I only have one word for this game....P-H-A-T, PHAT!

The Good
The commentary is nothing short of brillaint and there is practically no repitition. Be A Legend Mode is OK but it needs a little work. Also, no goaltending, out of bounds or fouls makes the game WIDE open. It is so competitive to win the game and get the most trick points.

The Bad
The only thing I didn't like was the blisters on my hand after playing it for so long.

The Bottom Line
As a must buy for any NBA or street ball fan or sports fan in general.

Description

Agent George, a CIA agent, is sent to Iraq and Kuwait to save the oil refineries in the Gulf and halt the production of chemical weapons. To stop Saddam's men, you collect bombs and weapons or battle it out with your fists and kicks. The side-scrolling levels all have a Middle Eastern theme.

Trivia

Title

The working titles and the game's name all refer to the military operation "Desert Storm" in Iraq. It was often on the news at that time and Color Dreams did everything they could to get it developed while it was still a hot topic.

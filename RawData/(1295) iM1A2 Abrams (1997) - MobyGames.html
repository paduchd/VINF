Description official descriptions

Similar to the M1 Tank Platoon games, iM1A2 Abrams is a tank simulation game focusing on battlefield management and commanding an entire company through three individual campaigns (Ukraine, Iraq and Bosnia).

Trivia

Title

iM1A2 Abrams is the successor to M1 Tank Platoon and would originally be called Tank Platoon 2 Tank Platoon was created in 1989 by 'Wild' Bill Stealy for MicroProse. When Stealy sold MicroProse in 1997 he could not claim the Tank Platoon title anymore and the game was called iM1A2 Abrams.

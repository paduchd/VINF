Description

A typical FPS set during World War II, Mortyr 2 is slightly related to the first Mortyr. This time, you play as the British intelligence officer Sven Mortyr who must investigate the enemy operations in Norway, his homeland. It looks like the Nazi forces are developing a super-weapon and the own father of Sven Mortyr (a famous physicist) is been forced to work on this project.

You will infiltrate the enemy fortress using a mix of historically accurate weapons with some experimental (or sci-fi) new ones, blasting through 11 single-player levels, to uncover the secrets of the project and rescue the scientist.

There are also 4 multiplayer levels available.

Trivia

German version

There a number of changes in the German version: * All blood was removed * Ragdoll effects on corpses were removed * All symbols associated with the Nazi regime were removed, e.g. swastikas, iron crosses, SS runes or imperial eagles. In one instance, there is a swastika left on a land map. * During localization, sentences like "Heil Hitler" or "Sieg Heil" were removed.

A detailed list of changes can be found on schnittberichte.com (German).

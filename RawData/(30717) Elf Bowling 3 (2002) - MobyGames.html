Description

Santa has returned to the North Pole but there is a problem. There always seems to be. It seems the reindeer have gotten into Dingle Kringle's hooch stash and gotten soused. Now they're out of control and Santa can't use them to pull his sleigh so he needs to train his elves to fly and land accurately. That's why Santa has taken one of Mrs. Claus' bras, hooked it between two trees and set up targets of the mountainside.

This is a target game, something like midget tossing or lawn darts. Taking an elf, you put him in the bra sling and you will see an arrow with a line moving up and down it. You aim the arrow left or right with the mouse. The moving line is the shot's power. When it is where you want it, press the left mouse button to fire. If you hit one of the floating balloons, you will get the point amount, depending on what balloon was hit. Starting in level two, if you hit a reindeer, you will be knocked from the sky, maybe landing on a target, maybe not. Also, starting with the second level, you will have presents. These will give points, protect from reindeer collisions, etc. You will have ten tries per level to get as many points as you can but you must get at least two bullseyes per level or the game will end.

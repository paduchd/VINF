Description official description

A group of do gooders, set on making the video game galaxy pure of evil, entrap all the villains of video games in picture patterns and it's up to the Boppin brothers to save them by matching all the pictures together, and destroying the blocks.

Trivia

Amiga version

According to the programmer's comments in the DOS release, due to their small-time publisher not understanding the declining Amiga market, the original version performed extremely poorly in the stores: "We sold a grand total of 284 packages of Boppin', one of which was an empty box."

DOS version

The DOS version has more levels than the original Amiga version and has improved graphics. There are several differences regarding gore, e.g. the Amiga version has a different logo, and when one of the brother's lose they will either shoot themselves in the mouth, or stab themselves in the heart. Information about how to restore this content can be found in the tips & tricks section.

Freeware release

Boppin' is now available as freeware. The version is fully XP compatible. Check it out here.

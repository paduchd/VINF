Description official descriptions

You take control of a monster truck (like Bigfoot) and compete against other drivers in various races. There are a few King-of-the-Hill type contests also. It doesn't have racing season option so you are limited to pick a race and go.

Cool trucks, cool tracks, cool music, cool game

The Good
I liked just about everything about this game. The trucks were really cool, and they behaved like a real monster truck would. The tracks were great, because they were creative, very original. I think that my favorite track was VooDoo Island. The ability to change the weather was cool. I liked the fact that you could race in any track you wanted, in any truck you wanted, any time you wanted. You didn't have to complete the first track to get to second track, and then earn other trucks, and so forth. The music was pretty good, too.

The Bad
I didn't like the fact that the scenery kept rendering itself every so feet away from me. It really took some of the fun of interacting with it away (what fun is jumping off a cliff if you can't see the bottom?).

The Bottom Line
The bottom line is this: If you like to get down and dirty, this game is for you. Most everything about this game is excellent; the gameplay, the music, the tracks, and, of course, the trucks.

Windows · by Mullet of Death (592) · 2002

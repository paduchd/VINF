Description official descriptions

The AoW Masters collection is a bargain priced compilation of the original Age of Wonders and its sequel Age of Wonders II: The Wizard's Throne. Both games are patched to the current version and include .pdf manuals. Also, in an ironic side effect of budget compilation packaging, the AoW disc includes the demo for AoW II.

Trivia

Disc misprint

Since this was a small run budget release title, I can only assume that my copy is not unique just because the discs are misprinted. AoW II is burned on the disc labeled AoW and vice versa. Both games work which is what matters but any collector interested in publishing goofs may want to keep an eye out for this set.

Description official descriptions

Airline Transport Pilot is an advanced simulator from subLOGIC, that allows you to fly airliners, such as Boeing 737, 747, 767 or Airbus A320. The graphics engine was improved, with two dozen different airports across the entire continental US, some buildings in major cities, and radio beacons all across the country. And for the first time in flight sim history, Air Traffic Control. The AI ATC will actually give you directions!

Trivia

Certificate

The original release provided no means to acquire the ATP certificate. The internal program flag that was to be set was never achieved during in-house and beta testing.

Lawsuit

This game's publication was delayed several months due to a lawsuit from Microsoft, whom by then owns some of the code that went into their Flight Simulator series, developed with subLOGIC and BAO. The case was eventually settled out of court, forcing subLOGIC to relinquish any mention of "flight simulator" from their name or any products they make.

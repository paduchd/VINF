Description official description

Super DX-Ball is the fourth game in the DX Ball series and the gameplay is similar to Breakout/Arkanoid, but with lots of power-ups that effect the paddle and the bricks. There are 4 different board sets: Retro, Fun, Challenge, and Super. Additionally, there are 2 hidden sets and 3 expansion board sets (Bonus, Surprise and Treasure, of which 2 are free to download). Super DX-Ball has bricks of different shapes including triangles and rounds.

Trivia

A month after Super DX-Ball was released, the same company released a free add-on for their other game Pocket Tanks, the add-on was a 5 weapons pack called Super Pack, and the news at the official Super DX-Ball site said: "To celebrate the release of Super DX-Ball, a brand-new 'free' weapon pack for Pocket Tanks Deluxe"

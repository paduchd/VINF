Description official description

Angelfish, a sequel to Firehammer, is a traditional top-down shooter. The Sirian armies raid the universe and the player has to destroy their mothership.

The game features hand-drawn graphics with 18 levels to play through. There are typical genre elements present, such as power ups, bonus multipliers and end bosses. The difficulty is designed to be a challenge for experienced shooter players.

Description

Flynn is a small dwarf protecting the world of dreams from evil creatures sent by a new pirate-tyrannic beast coming from nowhere. This new super villain sends goblins, kobolds and fat monsters to terrify small children while they are sleeping. Kill all the monsters by jumping on their backs or by throwing cards at their face in order to restore peace to the world of dreams.

Flynn's Adventures is a side scrolling game (Ghost'n Goblins like) with very simple controls. Use left and right (or 4 and 6 of your phone) to move, use up (or 2) to jump and down (or 8) to crouch). Use the fire button (or 5) to throw a card.

The player has to fight through ten levels of gameplay with five different graphic environments against a dozen of enemy types. These levels mix heroic fantasy art with a cartoon style.

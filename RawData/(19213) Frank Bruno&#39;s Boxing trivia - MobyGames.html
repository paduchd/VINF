Trivia

Loading Screen

The C64 version loading screen shows a television featuring four channel buttons, marked BBC1, BBC2, ITV1 and ITV2, like many TVs on sale in the late 1970s in the UK. The first three were the channels available in the UK from BBC2's launch in 1968, and for much of the 1970s ITV were expected to control the fourth available TV channel when it launched, however objections to a second purely commercial channel meant that this ultimately became the public-private partnership Channel 4. An entirely different digital (and later satellite) channel called ITV2 ultimately launched in 1998.

Description

Has everyone played the Rubik's Cube? More to the point, have you ever completed it without pulling it apart? Well, unless you can pull apart a CD-Rom and put it back together, the puzzles on this CD must be solved with the brain.

Rubik's Games includes the original Cube of joy, as well as smaller and larger versions. But there is plenty more then that:

Zigthough: A Tetris-style game where you must get a string of five colored blocks from one end of the screen to the other.

Rubik's Playground: you must get 5 different style balls into the exit hole, while obstacles get in your way.

Cover-Up: Unstack multiple tiles of colored blocks to fit the entire play area, but you can only lay the tiles out in straight lines from where the stack started.

Paint War: A turn based strategy game where you are trying to paint half the 42 squares on the board or destroy all your opponents game pieces. Multi-play is available for this as well.

Trivia

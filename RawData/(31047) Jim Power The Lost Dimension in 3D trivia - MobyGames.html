Trivia

Prototype

A Genesis version was being developed but it never passed the prototype stage.

Working title

Based on the emergence of a prototype, this game was apparently initially developed as part of TSR's ill-fated Buck Rogers XXVc line, with the intended title Buck Rogers: The Arcade Game. It is unknown why the license was not ultimately used.

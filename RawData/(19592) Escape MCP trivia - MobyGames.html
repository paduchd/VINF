Trivia

Development

Mike Blackman developed this game when he was 15, and it paid for his first car. He received no money from the UK release however.

Inspiration

In an unusual turnaround the game's background story is highly derivative of the then-recent movie Tron (also featuring an antagonist MCP or Master Control Program) while the gameplay does not draw on any of the movie's frequently-cloned game sequences.

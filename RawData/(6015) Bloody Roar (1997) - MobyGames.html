Description official description

Bloody Roar is a fighting game with a twist. At the press of a button, the player character changes into a beast of unique strength and ability over the character's normal attacks. Each character has their own beast to transform into, and most battles come down to how the player uses this transformation to their advantage.

The game features over-the-top special moves and combos in a 3D-like atmosphere.

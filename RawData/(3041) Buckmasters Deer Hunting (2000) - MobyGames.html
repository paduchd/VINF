Description official description

Yet another title from Wizardworks, the creator of Deer Hunter... This one let you hunt... more deer! Have outdoorsman Jackie Bushman guide you through the forests and teach you the basics in four different locations with a full complement of rifles, bows, and other authentic outdoor gear. Go online and hunt with friends over Internet multiplayer.

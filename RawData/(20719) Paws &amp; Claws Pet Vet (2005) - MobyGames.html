Description official description

In Paws & Claws Pet Vet, you are a veterinarian who has to build up his own animal clinic. You start with a small practice, treating bunnies, guinea pigs, and birds. Later on, you can expand your animal clinic and with continuing education you can specialize yourself to treat bigger animals such as cats, dogs and horses. The game mixes simulation and strategy aspects using a 3D engine. As a special feature, you can learn the art of horse whispering.

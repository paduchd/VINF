Description official descriptions

Fireworks are launching across a city's skyline. In this puzzle game, you must connect them quickly to have them explode. Earn more points for detonating more fireworks at the same time. You can also work against another player to see who can gain the most points. Be careful, as control of the screen can shift and one player could be left with few fireworks to explode.

Fantavision: Gorgeous graphics, disappointing game

The Good
The graphics in "Fantavision" are gorgeous, the sound will take you back to Fourth-of-July celebrations above the bay, and some of the graphical motifs are positively bizarre. I kept on playing mainly so I could see what new twists would be next.

The Bad
The gameplay is painfully repetitive. All of the elements in the entire game, aside from the various backgrounds, make their appearance in the first stage. After that, things just get more and more difficult. Among puzzle games I have played, "Fantavision" is just plain mediocre.

The Bottom Line
If you're curious, it's a great pawnshop buy for $5. Otherwise, don't waste your time or money. There are a lot of games that are a lot better.

PlayStation 2 · by Ryan Kelly (9) · 2005

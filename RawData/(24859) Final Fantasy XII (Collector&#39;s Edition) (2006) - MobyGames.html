Trivia

Distribution

The collector's edition of Final Fantasy XII was exclusively sold by EB Games and Gamestop, as well as Barnes and Noble through a partnership with Gamestop. Although it was exclusively sold by the retailers mentioned, this edition was created by Square-Enix and has no special retailer branding on the package.

Description official descriptions

A first person shooter powered by the Lithtech Jupiter engine wherein you take the role of an unknown lunatic only known as The Sniper, blasting this way through the streets killing everything he comes across. You though don't only shoot using a sniper gun and even most of the time you're blasting your way through with automatic pistols, shotguns and grenades, instead of the sniper rifle you might expect. The game contains a little RPG element which lets you develop your skills as you progress.

Worst Fps Game EVER!

The Good
mmm.....hard one. you play as a sniper ... and it similar in some ways to deus ex with the rpg elements which lets you develop your skills.. and that all...

The Bad
First of all this game uses the Jupiter engine (games like nolf,avp use it to) but this is the worst use of the engine i seen yet! Graphics sucks real bad!! it looks like a game from 97/96 not 02. the story is just stupid, AI is bad as it gets. and the gameplay feels like crap(really really bad!!). blood looks like a red color. animation done badly. .................................. poor,ugly game!

The Bottom Line
its even worse then Mirage Media other two games (and that says alot): Mortyr (2093 - 1944) & Rise of the Robots.... don't even think about trying this game unless your a sick, sick person who enjoys poor crappy games. i really felt like puking when i played this plague! again don't play it!!! 1/10 - (i was kind with the score here!)

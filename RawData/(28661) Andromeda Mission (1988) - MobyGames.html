Description

Somewhere in the far reaches of space, an epic battle is taking place.

As a lone hero, you fly your ship into the screen Space Harrier style, though a scrolling alien planet, shooting any enemies that approach. Pods offering improved weapons are unleashed regularly After you get through the waves of aliens, you must destroy a mother ship. Then you blast off into space, fighting your way to the next planet. Can you survive all sixteen planets in the Andromeda Mission? We will see.

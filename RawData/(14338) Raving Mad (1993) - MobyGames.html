Description

Three platform action games with a cutesy feel come together in this compilation:

James Pond 2: Codename: RoboCod, the second James Pond game in which our intrepid hero must prevent Dr Maybe from ruining Christmas.

Rodland, a conversion of Jaleco's coin-op in which fairies Tam & Rit must rescue their mum by using a stick to thump enemies against the ground

Mega Twins, another arcade game involving sibling, who must kill the dragon who murdered their Father the King.

Trivia

Commodore 64 version

The manual includes loading instructions for a C64 version, which has not yet been found but would be a crucial find, as Mike Agar and Wayne Billingham produced a C64 conversion of Mega Twins which is thought to have been highly impressive, but went unreleased as US Gold scaled back their 8-bit activities.

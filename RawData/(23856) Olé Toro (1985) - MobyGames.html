Description official description

OlÃ©, Toro is an early game (though not the first) dedicated to the Spanish tradition of bullfighting. Here you will play the role of the bullfighter, who is situated in the middle of the screen. He will have to avoid the repeated attacks the brave animal will try to commit against him by appearing on the right. For that, you should develop an accurate technique and learn the exact moment you need to press the keys in order to activate every move. Otherwise, you will most probably pass the next days in the Plaza's hospital.

Trivia

Ole, Toro has the "honour" to be the only game to have scored a 0 in the history of the "Computer & Videogames" magazine. But the most interesting part is that it was not because the game itself was bad, but as a protest due to its thematic. At the same time, Crash magazine received lots of letters of its readers criticizing their uncritical attitude towards the program scenario, including pearls like: "The people who created this game ought to have THEIR ears chopped off - and US Gold ought to be ashamed of themselves actually releasing it.".

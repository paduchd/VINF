Description official descriptions

Boogerman is a jump-and-run game where the protagonist flicks boogers and farts at enemies in order to stand a chance against the evil Boogermeister. The game features about 20 levels of dirt, slime and bad taste in worlds like "Mucous Mountains", "The Pits" and "Flatulent Swamps".

Boogerman -the most original superhero in videogaming

The Good
The characters in this game, that is, the enemies and the main hero are very well drawn and animated. In game characters are never standing still, they walk about, pick their nose, make a funny pose etc, generally being animated and alive. The sound and voice acting in the game were excellent for that gaming era. The hero spits, nose picks, farts all with sounds that are sufficient rude.

The environment in this game is bright and colorful. The game levels are well designed and interesting with the main theme being 'gross things', you have levels such as "Mucous Mountain" and "Flatulent Swamps" which the hero would have to go through nose cavities, slime and other such gross things. Each level exit is a toilet bowl, in which the hero jumps in and flushes himself down to the next level!

The Bad
Some of the levels are somewhat repetitive and samey.

The Bottom Line
A very humorous platform adventure set in a strange world with a hero who uses his nose pickings, farts and burps to destroy his enemies. The game is so well animated that it's like playing a cartoon character in a cartoon show.

Genesis · by BeaconBlue (15) · 2006

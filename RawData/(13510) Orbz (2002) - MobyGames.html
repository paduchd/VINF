Description

Orbz (unofficially known as Orbz 2 after version 2.0) is a combination of golf, Super Mario, and its own quirky style. You take an Orb, and must aim it and launch it at a designated level of power to collect stars as fast as you can. Collecting multiple stars in one launch multiplies your points. You will need to do this either yourself or against other Orbz, the latter of which can be experienced online as well.

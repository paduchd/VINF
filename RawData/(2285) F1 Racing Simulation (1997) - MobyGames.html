Description official descriptions

All Teams, Drivers and Tracks of the 1996 FIA Formula 1 Championship season are included. You can modify your car and after the races you even could learn not only from the replay - there were also telemetry dates saved.

The sounds of the engines were recorded from original Renaults.

Trivia

Jacques Villeneuve

Jacques Villeneuve does not feature in the game as his name was licensed to CART at the time of the game's release. His place is taken by a driver named "Driver X".

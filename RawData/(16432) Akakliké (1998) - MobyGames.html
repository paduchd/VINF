Description official description

This game for children from two to five teaches the basics of logic through twelve small games. You guide Akaklik√© through his home and go to sleep in one of the twelve chambers. In his dreams, you can solve the puzzles. Most of them deal with mouse movement, animals, shapes, rhythm and matching exercises with colourful photos. Some of the games have three difficulty levels.

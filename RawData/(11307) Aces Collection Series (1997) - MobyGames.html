Description official descriptions

Aces: Collection Series comes on 2 CDs with all the award-winning historical flight and naval simulators from Dynamix. A total of six games are included in this collection:

  • A-10 Tank Killer (v.1.5)
  • Command: Aces of the Deep
  • Aces of the Pacific and its add-on Aces of the Pacific Expansion Disk: WWII: 1946
  • Aces over Europe
  • Red Baron and Red Baron: Mission Builder
  • Silent Thunder: A-10 Tank Killer II

It also includes additional information about the people and planes that contributed to the advance of aviation between 1903 and 1939.

Trivia

Extras

This collection includes the war maps of the western front in the 'Dunkirk', 'Somme', 'London', 'Paris' and 'Verdun' regions. For Aces of the deep, a huge authentic looking war map is also included, which is in German!

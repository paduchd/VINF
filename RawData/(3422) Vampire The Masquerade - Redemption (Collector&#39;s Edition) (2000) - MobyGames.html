Description official description

This limited Collector's Edition of Vampire: The Masquerade - Redemption contains the game itself, the manual, a Limited Edition hardcover Book of Nod, a Vampire Camarilla Pendant and Bradygames' mini-strategy guide.

Trivia

German version

There's also a German special edition of the game. This edition has a coffin-shaped box. This enormous box (1m in size) contains the original game, an ankh and a t-shirt.

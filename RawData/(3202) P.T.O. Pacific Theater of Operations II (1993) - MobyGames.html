Description official description

PTO II is a KOEI game that depicts the conflict between the USA and Japan during World War II. The player takes control of the Navy forces of both nation, and tries to outsmart each other.

Loved this game

The Good
being in control of the war from the top. Picking and chosing my own stratagies, assigning production types.

The Bad
I wished the island land operations were a little more like Panzer general, where I could control land forces too. Wished i had control over military inventory needed for each battle. You know, being able to pick whether I needed tanks on Iwo Jima or more flamethrower tanks. Do I need hvy arty or light mortars. Heavily armed troops or light infantry. Needing to get supplies to the troops is another vital component. Would love to be able to have more control over assigning the production TYPES of ships, planes, tanks etc. Its decent now

The Bottom Line
This game was awesome when it came out in the early 90's, Imagine how good it could be TODAY in 2003 with more detailed control over each island campaign. Id like detailed battles for each island instead of the limited control now.

Windows · by david gates (2) · 2003

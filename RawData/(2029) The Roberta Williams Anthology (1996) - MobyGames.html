Description official description

The Roberta Williams Anthology is a compilation of games created by Sierra game designer and co-founder Roberta Williams. It contains:

  • the first seven King's Quest games
  • Hi-Res Adventure #0: Mission Asteroid
  • Hi-Res Adventure #1: Mystery House
  • Hi-Res Adventure #2: The Wizard and the Princess
  • Hi-Res Adventure #6: The Dark Crystal
  • Time Zone
  • The Colonel's Bequest
  • The Dagger of Amon Ra
  • Roberta Williams' Mixed-Up Mother Goose
  • the first chapter of Roberta Williams' Phantasmagoria
  • video interviews with Ken and Roberta Williams, and some behind-the-scenes footage of the making of King's Quest: Mask of Eternity

The Apple II titles contain an Apple II emulator for Windows that can run them.

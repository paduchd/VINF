Description official description

You must take to the tee, and get that little white ball in the hole in the least amount of shots. The new "quick start" feature allows you to get into the game quicker than before (skipping some of the more technical parts of the sport). You can create your own golfer (give him a face, a body, and a unique style of dress) and take him onto the pro tour or the amateur circuit. If your game needs a little work, the putting practice greens and the driving range are available.

Description

Truck dismount is the follow-up to Stair dismount. The aim of the game is to hurt a little fellow called DJ. After the incident with the stairs, he noticed he needs that kind of an activity to relax, so he signs up for a crash test dummy procedure. You place him somewhere near a truck and then try to smash him as painful as possible against a wall. You control the damage rate (which is awarded in points) through a number of parameters: the speed of the truck, two ramps, the position of the guy, ...

The game features different camera views, a replay function for top scores and a near unlimited number of possibilities.

It's sick, it's wrong, it's fascinating, it's shameful fun

The Good
As a way to waste a couple of minutes, this game is addictively fun, in the sick, macabre way that makes people slow down to look when they pass a highway accident.

The Bad
While there's a lot of flexibility in setting up the actors, I wish there were more options for controlling the camera.

The Bottom Line
It's sick, and perversely fascinating. I'd never pay money for something this sick, nor for something with such a short play time, but it's right up there with Joe's Cartoons for sick fun.

Windows · by weregamer (155) · 2003

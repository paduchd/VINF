Description official descriptions

Bomberman LIVE is a re-imaging of the original Bomberman game.

The game features frantic, explosive action with high-definition graphics, four-person local multiplayer and eight-person online multiplayer. Players can customize their own Bomberman with over 10,000 unique combinations. The game has the player move a character around in a maze, destroying enemies while planting bombs and collecting power-ups that increase the player's bomb radius, bomb power, speed, and more.

Description official description

BattleBots: Beyond the Battlebox lets you create the robot warrior of your dreams and challenge 16 actual bots from the TV show. Equip them with weapons and armor of your choice and challenge your friends to system link play with up to four players. Play solo against three bots simultaneously in Tournament or Brawl mode. Combat takes place in five different hazard filled arenas including the famed BattleBox. BattleBots: Design & Destroy captures the action and excitement of the TV show that started the robot combat craze.

Trivia

Version differences

The game originally shipped as BattleBots: Beyond the Battlebox, which was a 64MBIT cartridge with Save RAM. This version was quickly replaced with BattleBots: Design & Destroy which is the same game with some bug fixes, scaled back to a 32MBIT cartridge with no Save RAM (it uses a password save system instead).

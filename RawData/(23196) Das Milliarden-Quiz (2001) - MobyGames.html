Description

Das Milliarden-Quiz (The Billionaire Quiz) is a quiz game, quite similar to Who Wants to Be a Millionaire. It features about 1,800 questions (text and voice, no multimedia) . To win the game the player has to answer 15 questions out of 6 themes (geography, history, politics, fine arts, sports and entertainment) with increasing difficulty. There are 4 different types of jokers available for the player and an option to answer question within a time limit. The game is playable for up to 4 players (hot-seat, changing of player after 3 questions) and has a high score screen.

The game was only released in German-speaking countries and questions are all in German.

Description official description

Bubsy In: Fractured Furry Tales is the only Bubsy game to appear on the Atari Jaguar. It's a side scrolling platform game similar to the Genesis and SNES Bubsy series.

Bubsy is a bobcat superhero who can defeat all enemies by jumping on top of them. In Fractured Furry Tales, he finds himself in a series of fairy tales. When all havoc breaks loose in the land of fairy tales, Mother Goose sends in a brave and noble hero (who happens to be Bubsy) that will restore peace to the fairy tale land. So, Bubsy has to travel through 15 levels and set things right.

Trivia

Sudden change of plan

Bubsy In: Fractured Furry Tales was originally intended to be a Jaguar port of Bubsy in: Claws Encounters of the Furred Kind, the first Bubsy game. But the developers suddenly decided to make it a new game entirely.

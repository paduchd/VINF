To do your mission you are supplied with huge arsenal of realistic weapons, divided to six categories from normal pistols to machine-guns, rocket launchers and chain-guns including AK-47 and Gatling Cannon. El Matador also presents two Max Payne's style 춺slo-mo췉 modes. One is automatically activated, for instance when you dive, and doesn't cost anything. Trivia

Advertisement

The German publisher Frogster sold a Collector's Edition, basically just a German version without the changes specified in the trivia item "German version", exclusively at the Games Convention 2006.

German version

In the German version the story was rewritten so that the protagonist is no longer an official agent but a gangster and double agent. To illustrate that, there are numerous new cutscenes. There are also additional changes: * The MP5 and MP5 with silencer were re-labeled as tranquilizer guns and given unlimited ammo. The tranquilizer gun itself was made more effective. * Ragdoll effects were completely removed * It is not possible to shoot at corpses * Almost all blood (except the one of the protagonist) was removed * Some Nazi references in dialogues were removed

A detailed list of changes can be found at schnittberichte.com (German).

Description official descriptions

This game bundles 12 of the existing puzzles that can be found in The 7th Guest, The 11th Hour and Clandestiny (with each game contributing 4 of them), leaving out the story elements of the original games and instead focusing on the puzzles themselves. The puzzles can be accessed directly by clicking on any of the rooms of a dollhouse. An additional new 13th puzzle is available when completing all 12 of the old puzzles.

The included "Guestbook" contains short descriptions of the puzzles.

Trivia

Uncle Henry's Playhouse was one of the final games produced by Trilobyte. According to statistics released by PC Data, the game had sold a mere 27 units in the U.S. during the 1996 holiday season. It would eventually sell a total of 176 units worldwide. Shortly after the release of Uncle Henry's Playhouse, Trilobyte decided not to self-publish future titles.

Trivia

Music

The melody (composed by David Whittaker) from the "Stardust" area in Lazy Jones was sampled in the song Kernkraft 400 by Zombie Nation back in 1999. The song became a dance club smash hit and became popular at sporting events all over the world.

Development Details

All of the games were originally written in Basic, and then converted into machine code.

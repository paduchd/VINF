Description

The follower of the original Alien Phobia, this game is all about brainless shoot'em'up. You are up against the endless horde of aliens and you should take as many of them with you before they get you eaten. You can get powerups from the field which include better weapons(the basic rifle and three special weapons: Flamethrower, plutonium rifle and a cluster bomb launcher).

Are you ready to be eaten?

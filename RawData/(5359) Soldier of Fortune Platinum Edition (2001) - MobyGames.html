Description

This updated re-release of Soldier of Fortune includes:

  • all the content of the Gold Edition
  • new multi-player levels
  • GameSpy multiplayer support
  • an exclusive look at the sequel, Soldier of Fortune II: Double Helix

The retail version also contains bonus content on the game disc, such as artworks of game's characters and weapons, as well as the digital version of the manual.

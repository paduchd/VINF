Description official description

Moraff's Revenge is the first of Moraff's classic dungeon crawls. Choose to be a fighter or a wizard. The player runs about in four-color dungeons, returning to the top of the dungeon every once and awhile to climb up ropes into shops and inns.

The game features a map which fills in as more territory is explored. The "Beginner's Version" includes 17 levels before registering to have the full 70 levels available for play.

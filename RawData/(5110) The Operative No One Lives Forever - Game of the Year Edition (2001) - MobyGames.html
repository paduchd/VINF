Description official descriptions

In this Game of the Year edition, there are plenty of new extras as well as the original classic:

  • All 60 original assignments, as well as a completely new episode to play.
  • Multiplayer support for up to 16 players, with some new bonus skins.
  • The complete source code and map editor, allowing users to create and share their own mission and level creations.
  • CD Soundtrack, featuring all of the 60's style music from the game.
  • The official strategy guide from Prima, in case you need a hand

Trivia

The original German release of No One Lives Forever featured only English voices with German subtitles. For this budget edition the publisher made the effort to dub the whole game.

Description official description

From the people who brought you Deer Avenger and Panty Raider: From Here to Immaturity ... This parody has you, one bad bass, luring fishermen with lures such as suitcase of money, girlie magazines, and more.

Trivia

Underground Gamer

The back of the game's box includes a quote from Underwater Gamer regarding Bass Avenger as "The best game ever made." Underwater Gamer seems to be a fake publication, meant to further emphasize the fishing game parody.

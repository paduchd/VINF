Trivia

Bugs

The initial release of Myth II featured a bug which wiped out your hard drive when the game was uninstalled. A patch was released to correct it while the actual packages were recalled from store shelves.

Editors

The editors in Myth II were named "Fear" and "Loathing". One is for editing units, the other for terrain.

Soundtrack

The soundtrack for Myth II: Soulbligher was released in 1998.

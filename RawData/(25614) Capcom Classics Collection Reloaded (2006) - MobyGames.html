Description

The second PSP Capcom Classics Collection contains 19 Capcom games. Game sharing is supported and some can be played with up to three players. The following games are included:

  • 1942
  • 1943: The Battle of Midway
  • 1943 Kai
  • Commando
  • Eco Fighters
  • Exed Exes
  • Ghosts 'N Goblins
  • Ghouls 'N Ghosts
  • Gun.Smoke
  • The King of Dragons
  • Knights of the Round
  • Mercs
  • Pirate Ship Higemaru
  • SonSon
  • Street Fighter II: The World Warrior
  • Street Fighter II: Champion Edition
  • Street Fighter II Turbo
  • Super Ghouls 'N Ghosts (SNES version)
  • Vulgus

Any game in progress can be saved. While playing, you collect coins which can be taken into a slot machine to unlock music, concept art and cheats - 895 different items in total.

Trivia

Capcom Generations

All of the games in the compilation, with the exception of The King of Dragons, Knights of the Round and Eco Fighters, were taken directly from the Capcom Generations discs.

This is apparent because they have the same copyright information as the games on these compilations. The only change is an additional copyright notice for Capcom USA, since the compilations were never released in the US (with the exception of fifth compilation in the series).

The Street Fighter II games also keep the loading screens from Street Fighter Collection 2.

Trivia

Freeware release

This add-on was released as freeware. It can be downloaded at the 3D Realms site (www.3drealms.com)

German index

On April 30, 1996, Extreme Rise of the Triad was put on the infamous German index by the BPjS. More information about this topic can be found in the game group.

References

The level "Pykus Peak" is named after Joe Siegler's wife, Lynn Pykus.

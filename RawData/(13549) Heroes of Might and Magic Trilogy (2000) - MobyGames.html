Description

This compilation contains:

  • Heroes of Might and Magic
  • Heroes of Might and Magic II: The Succession Wars
  • Heroes of Might and Magic III: The Restoration of Erathia

Their respective expansion packs are not included.

Trivia

Release

This compilation was released some months after Heroes of Might and Magic: Millennium Edition. The only significant difference is that Heroes of Might and Magic II: The Price of Loyalty is missing.

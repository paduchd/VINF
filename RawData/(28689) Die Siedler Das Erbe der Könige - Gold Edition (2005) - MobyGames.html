Description

The German Gold Edition of Heritage of Kings: The Settlers contains the main program and the two expansion sets The Settlers: Heritage of Kings - Expansion Disc and The Settlers: Heritage of Kings - Legends Expansion Disc.

Additionally included is the original soundtrack, four new single-player maps and a collectable figure of the black knight "Kerberos". The knight can only be found in the first edition of this compilation.

Description

A football manager simulation game, Euro League's gameplay is a stat-crunching affair were you deal with every aspect of your team's campaign, be it on the Premier League, Third Division, or the UEFA cup. And speaking of your team, you can choose from practically every club in Europe, each with accurately modelled stats (1999-2000) and with every local tournament available (ie. the Spanish Super Cup).

Matches can be seen just as results, a ball-by-ball overview (without comments) or as a playable game were you control the action FIFA-style with simple pass/shoot/longball arcade controlls.

Multiplayer tournaments of up to 8 players also available as well as several difficulty settings to take some of the stat-related burden off newbies.

Description

Resembling Bejeweled 2, the iPod version of the popular puzzle game features special effects and animated backgrounds. The core game remains the same: matching up three or more of the same jewel by swapping their positions with neighboring jewels on a grid. Once the grid is clear, or a certain score has been reached, the player advances to the next level.

Trivia

Freeware version

There is also a free version of the game, released on 30th July 2007. iPod users can log on to popcap.com using their iPod and play a Safari-based version of the game. It was developed in cooperation with Arkadiusz Mlynarczyk.

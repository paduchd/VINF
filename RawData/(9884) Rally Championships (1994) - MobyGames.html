Description official description

In the game you buy one of the best rally cars, like Lancia Delta of Toyota Celica, drive it on different locations in time race. Driving on the track using accelerator, you collect money and other items. After the qualification you drive on a track to win a race and to get a prize. After that you can repair, improve, or even change your car using your money. The main goal is to win all championships.

Trivia

Amiga versions

Amiga have two versions of this game: separate for AGA and ECS chipset. In general the versions are the same game but, apart from the graphics, there some easily noticable changes: * Co-driver selection in both versions is different. The most expensive ones in ECS version are the least expensive in AGA version * In ECS version there are no bonus tokens on the track that can be collected. * AGA version contains countdown feature before the race while ECS version starts in the moment when the race track appears. * After race summary in AGA version is presented on the track screen, while in ECS version it is a completely separate black screen with white letters on it

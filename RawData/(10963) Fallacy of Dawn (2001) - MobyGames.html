Description official description

In a world that never quite got over the 80s, Delarion Yar dispenses quarters for the local arcade... badly. Trapped in the city of New Haz, heÂ´s crossed the wrong people one too many times and will do anything to get out.

Fallacy of Dawn is a modern-day graphical text adventure comedy within the cyberpunk genre. Featuring dozens of twisted characters and locations, this game merges combat and conversation in the classic Infocom style.

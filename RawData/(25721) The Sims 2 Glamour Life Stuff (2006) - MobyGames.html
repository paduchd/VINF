Description official description

Want to make your Sims live in the lap of luxury or want to make your other Sims feel jealous? You can add some glamour to your Sims' lives with this add-on.

In this add-on you get modern luxury furniture, extravagant d√©cor items for the bedroom, living room, and dining room. You can show-up the other Sims when you go out all dressed-up in the must-have fashions of the year, glitzy evening gowns, fur coats, and other formalwear. In all there are 60 items to spice up the life of your Sims.

This is the third "stuff" add-on in The Sims 2 series, adding new objects, clothing and decorations, rather than scenarios.

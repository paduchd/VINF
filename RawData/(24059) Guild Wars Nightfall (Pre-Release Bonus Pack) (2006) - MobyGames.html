Description official descriptions

The Guild Wars: Nightfall (Pre-Release-Bonuspack) is a retail bonus pack, which includes the following features:

  • Two pre-order bonus items, which are:
  • The Fiery Sunspear of Shelter for the Paragon

  • The Icy Soulbreaker of Enchanting for the Dervish

  • 1 character slot for free

  • A 14 days / 10 hours trial code to test Guild Wars: Prophecies and Guild Wars: Factions
  • A Bonus CD containing the following:
  • Lore about the new classes Paragon and Dervish
  • Trailers for Guild Wars: Prophecies, Guild Wars: Factions and Guild Wars: Nightfall.
  • Screenshots of Nightfall
  • 3 Nightfall-Wallpaper in 3 different high resolutions (1024*768 to 1920*1200)
  • Weblinks to the Official European Site and several European Fansites

Note: the first three items come as an activation key, which are requiring a functional Guild Wars: Nightfall account, or merged with Prophecies and/or Factions.

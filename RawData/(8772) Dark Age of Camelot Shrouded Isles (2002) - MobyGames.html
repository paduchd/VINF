Description

Dark Age of Camelot: Shrouded Isles is the first expansion pack available for Dark Age of Camelot players and requires the base game to play. It extends the story of the original game with new character classes, races, weapons, armor, outfits, monsters, advanced monster encounters, as well as several other additions. There are three new continents to explore and an upgraded graphics engine.

Description

In this freeware fanmade adventure, made with the AGS engine, Garfield is forced to break away from his everlasting eat and sleep routine when mutant lasagna takes over town. One of the Big Lab's chemical containers got lost during transport and spilled radioactive material turned all the wheat into strange creatures. The quest to restore the peace will take you through Garfield's town and beyond: the Yuck TV station, a park, outskirts and woods, Jon's house and some alleys. You will meet the familiar cast as well: Jon and his grandmother, the lovely Liz, Odie, Nermal, Arlene, Guido and many new characters.

The game uses 16-bit colours and stays true to the original drawing style and humour. You guide Garfield through a typical point-and-click interface where all possible actions are shown at the bottom. For convenience, they have been bound to keyboard shortcuts as well. The game stands out because of the many animations, the long gameplay time and some very original puzzles. A soundtrack, sound effects and a full saving system are included.

Trivia

Copyright infringement

Only four days after the release, PAWS Inc., who holds the copyright over Jim Davis' creations, forced System Error to take the game down, due to copyright infringement. As of 2014, you can still grab it from CWF-Freeware, find it in the related links section.

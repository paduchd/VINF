Description official descriptions

Land control is the main feature of this Anarchy Online expansion, along with additional graphic updates and new sounds. Vital for taking an active part in the conflict between the planet's two warring sides, you'll be able to gain personal and organizational benefits and advantages, including skill and experience bonuses, land-holder titles, and more.

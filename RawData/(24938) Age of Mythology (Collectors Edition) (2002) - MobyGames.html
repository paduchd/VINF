Description official descriptions

This Collectors Edition of Age of Mythology includes:

  • The Making of Age of Mythology on DVD
  • the original soundtrack on CD
  • six mini-posters showing the major gods of Age of Mythology
  • a movie-style poster with signatures of the Ensemble team
  • Bulfinch's Mythology book
  • three detailed technology trees
  • a limited edition Minotaur figurine

Trivia

Limitation

  • The American release of this Collector's Edition is limited to 50000 copies, as announced by Microsoft and Ensemble Studios.
  • The European English release of this Collector's Edition is limited to 21000 copies. You can find the serial number of each game on the back cover.
  • The German release of this Collector's Edition is limited to 13000 copies. You can find the serial number of each game on the back cover.
  • The French release (also available in Belgium) of this Collector's Edition is limited to 14000 copies. Serial number is found on the back cover.
  • The Italian release of this Collector's Edition is limited to 3000 copies. You can find the serial number of each game on the back cover.

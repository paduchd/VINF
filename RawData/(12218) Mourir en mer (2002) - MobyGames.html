Description

Mourir en mer is a short freeware adventure made with the Adventure Game Studio. A lonely young man has lost his mother and is locked up by his father in the attic. He is hideously disfigured and is never allowed to be outside the four walls. He finds comfort in Melville's Moby Dick and hopes to see the sea at least once in his life before he dies.

There is only a small amount of puzzles, but challenging enough to give you one hour of gameplay. Help the poor boy escape and find a way for him to see the sea.

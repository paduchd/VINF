Description

Metropolis is the city of the future, founded in 5067. You are a security agent for the software company IC&D and your adventure is about to begin. Solve ten different crimes and voyage the city through a series of "Zoomtubes". Just don't give out your M.U.M. code to ANYONE!

The game is largely conversation-driven, with a 20,000 word spoken vocabulary and advanced artificial intelligence.

Trivia

Innovations

Metropolis was the first PC game to feature full voice acting for most characters. Other games had small bits of digitized speech before, but not to the same level as Metropolis.

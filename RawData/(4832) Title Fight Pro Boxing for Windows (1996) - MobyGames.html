Description

This is an historical, text-based, boxing simulation. You can select practically any fighter (rated in a series of stats) from any classification and take on single matches against whoever you want. You select a basic strategy, venue, corner men and fight doctor, and then go at it. The battle is commented blow-by-blow, with a series of generic graphics popping up every now and then.

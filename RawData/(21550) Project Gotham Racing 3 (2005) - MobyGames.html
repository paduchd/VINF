Description official descriptions

Project Gotham Racing 3 combines the tried and true racing line of the series with an overhauled visual style and new game modes, both online and off.

Where as the previous titles had a range of vehicles spanning all of the possibilities, PGR3 sticks with the high end vehicles of Ferrari, Dodge, Lamborghini, 70 cars in total. A greater emphasis is on speed, as you drift around the corners of tracks the world over, building up your Kudos score and your overall rank.

PGR3 lives and breaths, however, online. Besides the usual multiplayer modes you'd expect, you can also upload and watch any world record beating scores and live replays of the latest online races.

Description

You'll wage spiritual warfare & defend yourself from the antichrist in this apocalyptic real-time strategy game based upon the LEFT BEHIND book series created by Tim LaHaye and Jerry Jenkins. Join the battle of Good versus Evil, leading Tribulation Forces or the Global Community Peacekeepers, and discover where all the people went.

The game story is essentially about the Biblical end of the world, written from the same perspective as the book series. Millions of people disappear from the world in an instant during the Rapture, and the world is plunged into the Tribulation - where the antichrist is building a one world government, and a one world religion. He is stamping out any resistance to his "peaceful utopia" - and the player's goal is to save as many people from his deceit as possible.

In multiplayer, you have the option of controlling the Global Community Peacekeepers - the antichrist's army.

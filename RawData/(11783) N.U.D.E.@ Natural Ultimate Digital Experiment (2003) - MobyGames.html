Description

In this game, you meet a robotic girl, named P.A.S.S, and you must teach her how to speak, name things, and teach her new words! She will understand everything you say,will engage in conversations with you,will learn from you, and lots, lots more! For example, the chair. Select the chair, and tell her "Chair". She will understand and remember that from then on.

You need to be able to speak japanese.

There is no nudity in this game.

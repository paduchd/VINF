Description

Four arcade-converted racing games feature in this compilation, named after a song by rock group Cream:

  • Chase HQ.
  • Hard Drivin' Power Drift Turbo Outrun

Trivia

Commodore 64 version

The Commodore 64 version of Hard Drivin' had never previously been released, though it was later issued on budget by The Hit Squad. It was not regarded as a good conversion, and the 6502 processor in the Commodore 64 wasn't really quick enough to accurately recreate the game.

"Great Idea Perfect"

The Good
If you are new to GTA prepare for the most fun addiction of sitting for hours and playing with the best storylines and graphics you seen, from starting as a nobody from out of town to ruling the city and also jacking racing cars; from watching tires get shot out and car doors falling off. I thought Rockstar did a very smart move by releasing GTA3 and GTA: Vice City in a first double pack for Xbox with new improved graphics and custom music playing from my Xbox system. These two titles were brought back perfectly after being on the PS2 console a while, on the Xbox they were made so great it's like playing a new game you can always go back and play them and not get bored and rebeat the game. Both games in the double pack are backwards compatible for play on the Xbox 360 so after you long rid of your original Xbox you can always play these perfect classics. The controls are so easy to figure out and play on Xbox and Xbox 360. Both games run more smoothly on the Xbox and less loading and more playing.

The Bad
Well there's nothing I didn't really like.

The Bottom Line
If you have never played a GTA game and own a Xbox, I highly recommend playing it. If you own a Xbox or PS2 or both I recommend buying this for your Xbox you will enjoy it more!

Xbox · by TigerFury007 (1028) · 2013

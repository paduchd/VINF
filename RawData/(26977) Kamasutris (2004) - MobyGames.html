Description

Kamasutris is a Tetris variant where all the blocks have been replaced by naked men and women in different positions. They enter the screen from the top, one by one, and can be turned around. The player needs to guide them to the bottom of the screen in a comfortable position to have intercourse with another "block". When that happens, a short animation is shown and the couple disappears from the screen, freeing up room. The game is over when the stack of people reaches the top of the screen. The game has 10 difficulty levels with increasing speed.

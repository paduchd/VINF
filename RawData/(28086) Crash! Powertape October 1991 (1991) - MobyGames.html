Description

Issue 93 of Crash! came with a cover-mounted cassette containing two full games: Doomdark's Revenge, Mike Singleton's continuation of the Lords of Midnight saga, and Spy Vs Spy, the first computer implementation of MAD magazine's characters. It also features a set of POKEs (cheats for games), plus Psychadelia, Jeff Minter's light synthesiser.

Trivia

Copyright

Crash! Issue 93 was the last one published by Newsfield before it folded - Europress later bought and relaunched Crash! and its' C64 counterpart Zzap! This could be the reason why the back segment of the tape inlay (see cover scan) has a blank space where the copyright information usually goes.

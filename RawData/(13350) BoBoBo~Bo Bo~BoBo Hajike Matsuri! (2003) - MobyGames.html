Description

In the year 300X, the evil Maruhage Empire has taken over the world, and Emperor Tsuru-tsurina IV wants only one thing...

SHAVE THE WORLD BALD!!!

As the deadly Hair Snatcher Army wreaks havoc upon the world and hair everywhere, a hero must rise to stop the mad plans of Tsuru-tsurina IV...

That man is BoBoBo~Bo Bo~BoBo, defender of hair and wielder of the deadly art of True Fist of the Nose Hair!!

Join Bo~BoBo and his friends Beauty, Don Pachi, Softon, and Heppokomaru as they battle the Hair Snatcher Army and keep hair everywhere safe from evil!

This game is based on the manga by Yoshio Sawai.

Trivia

Merchandise

BoBoBo~Bo Bo~BoBo has proven so wildly popular in Japan that (of course) the merchandise craze hit HARD with Bo~BoBo goods, including an anime series, video games, singles, and toys, toys, toys galore!

Tsuru-tsurina

Emperor Tsuru-tsurina's name is a play on words: "tsuru-tsuru" is the sound of something slippery in Japanese manga and anime.

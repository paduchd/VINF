Description official descriptions

IL-2 Sturmovik: 1946 combines all the previous releases of Oleg Maddox's IL-2 Sturmovik series simulating WWII aerial combat, along with new add-ons:

  • IL-2 Sturmovik
  • IL-2 Sturmovik: Forgotten Battles
  • IL-2 Sturmovik: Forgotten Battles - Ace Expansion Pack
  • Pacific Fighters
  • IL-2 Sturmovik: Pe-2 Peshka (v4.05 - previously download-only)
  • Sturmoviks over Manchuria (v4.06 - also for download)
  • 46 (v4.07 - also for download)
  • A detailed 450 pages long Plane Guide (UK only)
  • Making-of videos (UK only)

It also includes all the patches up to 4.07. Technically, Pacific Fighters is a standalone game, but most user-made campaigns and online servers require a merged install with the regular series. The 4.06 and 4.07 add-ons were not available for download prior to the release of this compilation.

The second DVD contains bonus content with art, screenshots, cockpits, ground vehicles and various photographs of Storm of War: Battle of Britain.

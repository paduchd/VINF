Description

In this platform game, you play Pekka, a rooster from Finland set out to rescue the chickens that were stolen from his farm. Using his doodle horn, eggs, and other goodies, he must travel across Rooster Island, winding around farms, caves, and castles while defeating various nasty little critters.

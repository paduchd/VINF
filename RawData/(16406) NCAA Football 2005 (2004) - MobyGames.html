Description official descriptions

NCAA Football 2005 is the latest in EA's counterpart series to Madden. Once again you are given the charge of earning a national championship for your college program. Once players graduate, you can export the draft class for later use in Madden 2005. Play online on Xbox and PS2 to challenge gamers around the world.

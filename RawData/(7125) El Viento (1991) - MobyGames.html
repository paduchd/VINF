Description

In 1930s New York, a dark cult plans the resurrection of their master, the Dark Prince Hastur, in order to control all of New York and the world. The mob is on their side in this, funding their dark projects and rituals needed to bring their dark master out into their world.

The only one that can stop them is a young girl named Annet... born of the cursed bloodline of Hastur, yet against his plans. Her only weapon: a mystic boomerang known as El Viento...

As Annet, you will do battle with the mob, navigating through Sewers, Caves and buildings, collecting bonus items along the way.

The Good
Nice story, varied gameplay, innovative boss design, unique weapon/magic use, nice graphics, perfect level of difficulty . . . a class act.

The Bad
I'm at a loss. It was a great Genesis game. I didn't feel compelled to play it again once I beat it, but I had a good time.

The Bottom Line
Side-scrolling shooter with minimum thought required. No puzzles, which was a plus for me, but you had to figure out your enemies at times to get past them.

Description official descriptions

LEGO Bionicle: Tales of the Tohunga is an action adventure game where players become the brave young Tohunga , completing several goal-oriented quests in six elementally-themed areas to discover the six Stones and summon the Toa. More than 20 levels . Six additional multi-player Tohunga athletic games â€“ themed to each of the six locations. In typical LEGO fashion, players can customize their Tohunga characters, choosing from a variety of masks, biomechanical limbs and torsos. The game is linked to the Bionicle play materials line. Players may enter special codes found on the play materials packaging to the game to gain access to secret game-enhancing features.

Trivia

Cancelled sequel

Another game based on the Bionicle series was supposed to be released for Windows, which would have some form of connection with its Gameboy Advance cousin. Unfortunately, the game was canceled due to production difficulties.

Title

The term "Tohunga" was later replaced by "Matoran" after complaints of the Maori, native people of New-Zealand.

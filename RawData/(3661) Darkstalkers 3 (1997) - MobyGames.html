Description official descriptions

This is the 3rd in the Darkstalkers series. It plays much the same as the first, but features better animation, more playable characters, and a deeper storyline.

Jedah, the "Lord of Vampire", has summoned all of the Night Warriors to his realm to be assimilated into his being. Of course, this serves as a great excuse to have more one-on-one fights...

Play as Jedah, B. B. Hood, Q-Bee, Lilith, Demitri, Morrigan, Anakaris, Victor, L. Raptor, Hsien-Ko, J. Talbain, Felicia, Rikuo, Sasquatch, Bishamon, Donovan, Pyron or Huitzil, and conquer the enemy.

Trivia

Arcade version

Four characters not found in the original arcade Vampire Savior (but in its pseudo-sequel Vampire Savior 2) made it to the home versions of the game: the robot guardian Huitzil (Phobos in Japan), the alien Pyron, the jiangshi Hsien-Ko (Lei-Lei in Japan) and Dark Hunter Donovan Baine.

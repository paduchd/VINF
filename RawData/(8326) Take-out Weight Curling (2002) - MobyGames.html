Description official description

Finally, the first full 3D simulation of Canada's national sport. Often called "shuffleboard on ice", curling is a game of strategy where teams slide stones down a long stretch of ice in an attempt to land them as close as possible to a bullseye target. If you didn't watch the 2002 Winter Olympics, go rent the movie "Men With Brooms", then you'll understand.

Take-out Weight Curling features team creation, tournament play, variable ice conditions, a choice of national or international rules, and even Gamespy supported internet play.

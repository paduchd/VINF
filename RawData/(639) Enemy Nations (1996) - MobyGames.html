Description official description

In Enemy Nations you can choose a race of that you'll be the leader - there's a great amount of them, mostly of course alien, but also the earthlings can be chosen. After this you land on a randomly created terrain with your landing ship. Your task is to conquer a planet for your race. Of course you're not alone, other races try the same. The game is basically a real-time strategy game, but with an important economic aspect, resource management is very important. The game has no campaign mode, all missions are stand-alone.

Great mixture of C&C and Transport Tycoon

The Good
The game has a nice complexity that isn't too hard. It isn't so boring like other strategie titles that have a resource managment. From the first arrive on a planet you must manage your resources.

The Bad
The grafix are really crap. The sound isn't really good, except of the industrial/techno music.

The AI of the opponents isn't really challenging. But you can choose how intelligent the enemies are. On hard, they build very fast, so the game can be really hard.

The Bottom Line
When C&C is too boring for you and you want to more to build an manage in your base, this game is for you. The grafix aren't the best, but for a real strategie gamer this isn't necessary.

A game that is really worth to play, you can get it from the homepage for free!

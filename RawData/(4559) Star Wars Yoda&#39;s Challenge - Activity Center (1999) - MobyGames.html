Description official description

Star Wars: Yoda's Challenge - Activity Center is a game for children that helps build and sharpen skills in math, music, and reading.

Players embark on six challenging missions with varying levels of difficulty. When all difficulty levels have been completed, a printable Certificate of Completion is issued to the player by Yoda, the Jedi Master himself.

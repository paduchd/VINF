Description

The Deluxe European edition of City of Heroes includes, besides the original game:

  • A digest of three printed issues of the City of Heroes comic
  • Printed comic by "PvP"-artist Scott Kurtz
  • Map of Paragon City
  • Exclusive in-game content (VIP badge, Prestige Power Slide power and unique cape)
  • A 'Buddy disk' 30 day free trial for a friend

Trivia

European version

The Deluxe edition of City of Heroes was released in Europe with a combination of components found in the US Collector's Edition and the original edition. The European master includes the first three 'Issues' of City of Heroes - the free expansions released in the US before European release - plus Scott Kurtz's PvP comic, and a digest of the first three issues of the Blue King Studios' City of Heroes comic book.

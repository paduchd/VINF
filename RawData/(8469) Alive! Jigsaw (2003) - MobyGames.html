Description official description

Ever desired to have animated jigsaw puzzles instead of static ones? This game gives you such opportunity. You will be able to take any AVI or MPG movie or short film clip (or static pictures) and turn it into an jigsaw. This feature can be turned off, if you prefer a more classical approach. The game has options that make it suitable for kids and those who prefer less challenge and animated images.

If you drag the piece close enough to its counterpart, they will automatically snap together.

Shareware restriction

Alive! Jigsaw has a unique method for making sure you buy the game after the free download. After three days of playing, images will be gradually darkened until you purchase the game.

Description

This edition is limited to 17,777 units worldwide and contains:

  • S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Shadow of Chernobyl
  • Special packaging
  • Survival guide with detailed information about the factions, enemies and weapons
  • "S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Shadow of Chernobyl - Part I - Deathzone" - a book telling the background story to the game by Claudia Kern & Bernd Franz
  • Bonus DVD with making-of movies, additional background on the real Tschernobyl zone, the official soundtrack, wallpapers and many more extras
  • Map of the complete in-game zone
  • Two exclusive multiplayer maps (Swimming Pool and Dark Valley) and four exclusive multiplayer skins (British, American, French and German military)

Trivia

If you manage to go to the military barracks located at the very south of the map (just before the starting camp), you can see a working television projecting a couple of in-game scenes in there.

Description

Pursuit of Justice is a Legacy Interactive murder mystery casting you as the district attorney investigating and prosecuting the murder of a young gang member. The game is mostly full-motion video and photo interaction. Contains two stages: the investigation stage, and the trial stage.

The opening video shows you the circumstances of the murder; your responsibility is to gather enough evidence to prove it in court. You have four days of game time to gather evidence, order tests, and find witnesses to prove the charge of first degree murder.

Once the trial starts, you'll call your witnesses, ask the right questions, object to the defense lawyer's questions, and use proper legal reasoning to respond to his objections.

Trivia

Pursuit of Justice was originally released in 1997 for on-line ordering only as an individual case called The Rat Tattoo Murder. It's first appearance on retail shelves was in the bundle called D.A. Pursuit of Justice.

Description

Runesword II is an RPG designed to appeal to "pen and paper" role-players. The game comes with two full-length adventures, a number of smaller adventures, and a completely flexible construction set. Though the adventures which come with Runesword II have a fantasy flavor to them, the construction set gives users complete control over every possible aspect of the game, allowing for any genre of RPG (sci-fi, horror, modern-day) to be created.

Trivia

Since Crosscut Games doesn't have a major publisher for this game, the game is only available via Shrapnel's Online Shop at www.shrapnelgames.com.

The game comes without any packaging - only a single CD-ROM in a paper-envelope and the 165-page manual.

Description official description

The Collector's Edition of The Lord of the Rings: The Battle for Middle-earth II includes:

  • A bonus DVD with HD trailers, cinematic movies, behind-the-scenes footage, developer interviews, an extensive collection of game art and the game's full music tracks.
  • A War in the North campaign poster, demonstrating the advance of both Good and Evil campaigns.
  • In-game bonus exclusives including skirmish/multiplayer maps such as Weathertop and Argonath, Create-a-Hero options that identify the player as a Collector's Edition owner in-game, and a unique Dragon unit skin.

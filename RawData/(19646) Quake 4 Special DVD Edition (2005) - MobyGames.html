Description official description

The special DVD edition includes:

  • Quake 4
  • Quake II
  • Quake II Mission Pack: Ground Zero (Q2 expansion)
  • Quake II Mission Pack: The Reckoning (Q2 expansion)
  • Concept and production art gallery
  • The making of: behind-the-scenes documentary.
  • Interviews with id Software and Raven Software

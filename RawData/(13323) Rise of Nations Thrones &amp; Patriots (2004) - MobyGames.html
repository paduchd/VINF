Description official descriptions

Rise of Nations: Thrones & Patriots is the expansion pack to Rise of Nations. In this version, the game introduces new features such as 6 new races that all have unique national powers and special units:

  • The Iroquois
  • The Lakota
  • The Americans
  • The Dutch
  • The Persians
  • The Indians (India)

Additionally, there are 20 new units, 3 new wonder buildings, and 4 new campaigns, containing over 130 scenarios which are of the following:

  • Alexander the Great
  • Napoleon
  • The New World
  • The Cold War

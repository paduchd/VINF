Description official descriptions

This is soccer, the Sega way. Instead of the usual technical football we are used to, Soccer Slam presents 3 on 3 soccer with a collection of crazy, over the top characters and special moves to create a non-stop action atmosphere.

Using elements similar to EA Sports' NBA Street, Soccer Slam also contains power ups to increase the zany gameplay even further. Game modes include Quick Start, the Continental Cup Quest plus a practice mode and a Tourney multiplayer mode.

Sega's Strange Characters+Soccer+Slamming+LOADS OF ACTION= SEGA SOCCER SLAM!!

The Good
Umm... Fabulous graphics, a great, innovative and strange idea from Sega, lots of action, a host of interesting characters, good training games, fabulous "killer" & "spotlight" kicks, many teams to choose from, lots of different options and competitions, great stadiums, and less players to control, I wouldn't say this game is better than the EASport's FIFA Series, I'd just say it was a refreshing change!

The Bad
Almost nothing, well, actually nothing. Except one thing: Why is there no commentary? That would be great! "And he's slammed him to the floor, he's taken the ball, he's kicked to outer space, the other team has the ball, she's got it, it's A GOAL!!!"

The Bottom Line
Very Unusual, very strange, very WEIRD, and awfully, I mean REALLY weird.

All that in a great SEGA way!

Sega delights once again!

GameCube · by Jim Fun (207) · 2002

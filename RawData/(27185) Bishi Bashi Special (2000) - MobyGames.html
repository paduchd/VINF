Description

Bishi Bashi Special is a compilation of the two Konami games Super Bishi Bashi and Hyper Bishi, which were originally released only in Japan.

This game is made up of 85 mini-games that are meant to be played against each other. Some of the games feature such things as body builders on pogo sticks jumping for a piece of meat, a dance-off to get the biggest afro, a bride throwing wedding cake down the aisle, juggling cavemen eating different colored balls, to name just a few.

The game can be played in single player mode or against up to 8 others. It's totally random who each player faces in each round of party play, and the game only allows for 3 controllers at the same time, so players have to be quick passing them to the current players.

Trivia

Website

Konami made a site dedicated to Bishi Bashi Special before the release of the game in Europe. Here fans could read interviews with the creator, win Konami PSX games, send in their very own fan photos, take a "scientific" test to see how compatible they are with the game before buying it... but best of all! There's a totally new mini-game made in flash called Elvis Game where you have to help Elvis eat burgers and fries while keeping an eye on his waistline that wasn't included in the PSX release.

It was available at http://212.38.95.19/bishibashi/flashintro.htm

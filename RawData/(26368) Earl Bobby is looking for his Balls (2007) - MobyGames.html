Description

The worlds most famous Scottish Earl continues his quest for the sense of life. And this time he is looking for something even more important than his shoes. This time he is looking for his balls...yes, his balls.

This is the second part of the Earl Bobby trilogy and it is much longer than the first one. It is a classic point-and-click adventure that features an inventory screen and several cursor modes like talk and look.

After Earl Bobby managed to find his shoes and many other things in his previous game, he went on the golf course and decided to play a match of golf. After he makes his first stroke one of his golf balls gets caught by a bird and the bird flies away. So he has a hard time figuring out where his ball is.

The game features many weird puzzles and crazy characters like Tiger Woody the golf professional, Baron Mucki the millionaire and Lula the nymphomaniac girl. Earl Bobby has to convince them to help him with his problems. Next to that, the game contains interactive golf passages and an interactive driving range simulation.

Trivia

Inspiration

Earl Bobby is based on the Austrian joke figure Graf Bobby (usually translated to "Count Bobby") who is still very popular in Vienna. Some movies were produced about him in the 60s.

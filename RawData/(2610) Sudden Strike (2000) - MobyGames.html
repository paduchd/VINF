Description official descriptions

This game offers the player the option to replay WW2 in a very realistic way. First you have to choose from three nations, then you go straight to the front lines.

The game is very tactics-based, as you won't be successful just by rushing in. Many details are everywhere and they are also take effect on the gameplay: Slightly damaged bridges can be repaired, ammunition is limited, soldiers can hide in houses or jump out of planes, etc.

There's also a variety of modes for multiplayers.

Trivia

CDV Software Entertainment, Sudden Strike's publisher, was surprised when sales of the game hit the roof.

It sold more than 600,000 copies and achieved the No. 1 ranking in the German GfK charts as well as No. 1 in France and No. 4 in England. The game was also honored with the Platinum Award by the VUD.

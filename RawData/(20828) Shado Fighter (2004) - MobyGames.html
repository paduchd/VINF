Description

Shado Fighter is a combat game where you can play one of the 7 characters of the game within 7 different fight arenas. This game is a Street Fighter 2 clone where many of the basic movement are available (combos, combinations, special movements, light and heavy hits). The game contains 3 different game modes (Story, time attack and survival) plus a practice mode where you will be able to learn the different moves of each character. In story mode, you will face all opponents once in his own arena until you face the boss (Shado himself). You will also be able to play with this final character by defeating Shado with all the other characters. In Time attack mode, the goal is to defeat the opponents in the minimum amount of time. In Survival mode, you have only 1 energy bar (refilling a bit between matches) to defeat as many opponents as possible. To win against each opponent the game will propose you, you will have to defeat it twice. To control the player, you have to use all buttons from 1 to 9 of your phone.

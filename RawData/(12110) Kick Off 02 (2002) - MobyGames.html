Description

A remake of the classic Amiga game Kick Off 2 made by Steve Screech, one of the creators of the original, with big help from fans that posted their ideas for the new game at ko-gathering.com, ideas that later were implemented by Screech. The game still has the fast paced gameplay the original was famous for, but with improved graphics and new features such as a team editor.

Description

Taking its cue from a notorious scene courtesy of director Peter Jackson's zombie splatterfest Dead Alive, this game puts the player in control of a man striving to collect keys and progress through levels by exploiting a radical new gameplay mechanism -- releasing some gory red fluid contained under high pressure in shambling zombies with the assistance of a lawn mower, then maneuvering through the slurry (sometimes with propulsion assistance from the mower blades) to gain access to higher platforms and, eventually, the missing Barbara.

Development

In addition to his usual self-imposed constraints, the author piled on some additional ones (including an unusual aspect ratio of 128x96 and a focus on the use of the Xbox 360 controller) in order for the game to qualify for Kokoromi's Gamma256 project.

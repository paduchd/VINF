Description

Double Digger is a multiplayer remake of the classic Digger game, one of the most known games in the history. The idea is the same, drive your miner through the haunted underground mines and collect gems and treasures while avoiding ghosts. This remake includes various new skins and modes of gameplay, very configurable game options, a saving and auto-saving feature (plus a special difficulty level for kids). Some more new ideas introduced in this remake are impossible-to-dig stones, new bonuses such as slow-down-enemies bonus and more. Fun for all classic Digger fans, and lets face it, who isnt?

Trivia

Differences

Although this remake of Digger added various things, it also left out some basic stuff: Remember how in the original Digger you could push yourself below a sack of gold, stopping it from falling down while pushing up. when doing so you could press F1 and shoot, only the shot would go backwards since you're pushing up, killing an enemy coming from behind. You cannot do that in Double Digger, you cannot support a sack of gold at all.

Description

The freeware game Return to Sector 9 is the sequel to Ambush in Sector 9 and offers 9 different game modes based around 2D arcade sci-fi shooting gameplay. Each game mode offers a background story and has different controls, using both the mouse and keyboard, offering the player full movement or the control over a stationary turret. The game types consist of multiple stages with different waves of enemies, finished off with a game boss.

Players can unlock up to 15 different ships and paint jobs, as well as the different game modes. During gameplay, there are both offensive and defensive power-ups, extra lives and a special weapon to clear a large part of the screen. High scores can be submitted to an online database to compare your ranking against the best players in the world.

Contest

The GMG contest winners referenced in the credits participated in a Game Maker contest (announced 10th August 2005) to create a ship for the game. The first three had their ship included in the game and the winner received a $20 Amazon.com voucher or a Game Maker registration.

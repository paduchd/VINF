Description

Super Bomberman is the first Bomberman game on the Super NES. Like most games in the Bomberman series, Super Bomberman features a normal game as well as the famous battle mode.

In the normal game, Mr. Karat and Dr. Mukk have been terrorizing Diamond City. You must go from board to board, defeating the bad guys and solving puzzles to move to the next board.

In battle mode, up to four players go head to head in a battle to the death. Players can be computer controlled or human controlled via the Super MultiTap.

A terrific game that you're guaranteed to enjoy!

The Good
What did I like? Oh boy, 6 world's, 8 levels in each world, lots of cool new bad guys in each world and a boss on the eighth level of each world. Set bombs out and blow up your enemies. Die and you get a password, which will take you back to the level you died on. Get power-ups, like roller skates, which make you move faster, flame icons, which make your bombs bigger, the abilities to punch and kick your bombs and to get time bombs which go off when you want them to. The end boss is really awesome and the music is quite good, especially the ending music! The controls are really simple and easy. You can play the story mode by yourself or with a friend. But wait, that's not all! In addition to all of the above there's also a Battle Mode where you get to chose an arena and compete in a battle between you and up to three other friends and/or computer player's!

The Bad
After you get good enough at it you can beat the story mode in one sitting easily - it takes about 2 hours. But it's still fun and Battle Mode is always fun. Also, although overall the music is good a few of the songs can drive you mad after a while.

The Bottom Line
Just get it already, if you don't already have it, trust me, it's awesome!

SNES · by darthsith19 (62) · 2006

Description official description

Super Burnout is an arcade-style superbike racing game inspired by Super Hang-On. Players control an ultra-fast motorcycle through races that take place on famous tracks throughout the world. There are six bikes to choose from with races on a total of eight different tracks. The usual racing game modes are present. There is a single race mode, a practice mode, and a championship mode. Players can also compete against a friend in a single-race versus mode.

Trivia

Title

The game was originally developed as Burnout, but according to Olivier Nallet, Atari's marketing department decided that adding "Super" would increase the number of sales.

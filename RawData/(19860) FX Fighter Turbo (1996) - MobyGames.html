Description

Fight a series of adversaries in one-on-one combat to save the universe from Rigel, again. This sequel to 1995's FX Fighter adds 2 new characters, new moves, environments, costumes, special effects, windows support, and network play, to the one-on-one 3d fighting game.

This PC original title supports 1 player vs AI, 2 players on the same machine, 2 players on an IPX LAN, and 2 players connected via modem.

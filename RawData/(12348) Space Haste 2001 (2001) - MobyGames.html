Description

Space Haste 2001 has similar gameplay to its predecessor. The player drives a hovercraft in a race and increases his top speed based on the performance. Winning races provides the player with bonus laps in which the previous time must be beat. This sequel features updated graphics.

Trivia

Title change

The game was originally released as Space Haste 2001 in, well, 2001. Strategy First picked up version 2.0 of the game in 2003 and published it as Space Haste 2 under their Big City Games label.

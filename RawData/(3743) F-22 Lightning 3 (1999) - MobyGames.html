Description official descriptions

Take to the skies with NovaLogic's latest version of their best-selling flight sim F-22 Lightning 3. Connect and play with up to 128 other pilots on the NovaWorld FREE hosting service. You can engage in dogfights with people all around the globe, or meet friends online to protect your airbase and destroy your enemies in Raptor Air Wars.

Trivia

Bombs

F-22 Lightning 3 was the first flight simulator ever (at least the first MAJOR sim) to include the B-61 tactical thermonuclear bombs (yup, nukes) as part of your arsenal, and yes, you get to use them.

Extras

Some packaging of the box includes a headset microphone, for use on Novaworld online game service.

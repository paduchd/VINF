Trivia

Compilation

This game is a compilation of the following previously released titles: * Arcade's Greatest Hits: The Atari Collection 2

  • Arcade's Greatest Hits: The Midway Collection 2

  • Arcade Party Pak

  • Williams Arcade Classics

All games from these titles are included except for BurgerTime and Moon Patrol, originally on Arcade's Greatest Hits: The Midway Collection 2, with the addition of Satan's Hollow and Rampart, not in any of the previous titles.

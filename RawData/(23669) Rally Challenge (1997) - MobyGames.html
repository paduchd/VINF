Description official description

Rally Challenge is a rally game featuring three vehicles: the Proton Wira, the Toyota Celica GT4 and the Subaru Impreza WRX. Gameplay is based on running a rally stage - a twisty section of road with snow, mud, asphalt or dirt. The player can choose to run a single stage, a "ghost race" against a previous run, or a championship season.

For the championship, you race all of the stages available against up to 9 other players - either human competitors or computer AI. As the races are done without any other cars on track, it is done by turn-by-turn. The game also offers a replay mode with the ability to save. Through a serial link or over a network, you can race against another human player.

Trivia

Rally Challenge had a Custom Notes editor that allowed you to create your own racing notes in .wav format. The notes would be read to you along the track (by your simulated navigator) while driving. The notes that came with the game were read in an Australian accent.

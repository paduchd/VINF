Description

Released exclusively through SegaSoft's Heat.net service, this is quite possibly the first online fighting game. Similar to games like Street Fighter 2 and Mortal Kombat, Net Fighter involves controlling an on-screen character, using special moves and attacks to beat your opponent. The game features eight playable characters, each with his or her own unique fighting style and set of moves/combos/special attacks.

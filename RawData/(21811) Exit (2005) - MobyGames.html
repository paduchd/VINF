Description official descriptions

Exit places you in the role of Mr. ESC, professional escape artist extraordinaire. As Mr. ESC, your job is to guide scores of people from precarious situations. In the midst of situations such as a burning apartment, you will guide and direct those people in an effort to take each and every person in each level out of harm's way. One hundred levels are available in the main quest, with an additional one hundred becoming available through Wi-Fi downloads.

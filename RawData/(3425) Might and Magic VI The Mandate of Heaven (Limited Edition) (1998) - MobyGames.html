Description

This compilation includes the following:

  • Might and Magic VI: The Mandate of Heaven on two Collector's Edition Holographic CD-ROMs
  • The complete Might and Magic I through V series on a CD-ROM, including printed versions of the original Maps and PDF manuals.
  • Exclusive Might and Magic VI mini-strategy-guide
  • Cloth map of Enroth

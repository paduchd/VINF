Description official descriptions

Fuzion Frenzy 2 is an arcade-action party game with over 40 futuristic mini games, from sports challenges to thrilling battles. Fuzion Frenzy is the name of a world-wide TV-show in which players competed to become the Ultimate Victor, win a huge amount of prize money and save the planet in the process.

The tournament now has game zones on seven planets across the galaxy. The six most famous contestants from previous tournaments return to the challenge.

The game has an offline multiplayer mode for competing against friends or family as well as an online multiplayer mode via Xbox Live. Tournaments can be customized to include only those mini games the player prefers.

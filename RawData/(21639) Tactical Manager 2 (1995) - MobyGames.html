Description official description

The sequel to Tactical Manager goes much further than the predecessor, including a massive database with 30 championships with 60 leagues, with over 32000 players.

The game runs from a regular Windows interface, and all options can be selected with the mouse or the keyboard. Game options include the usual tactical selection, sending the player to train weaker parts of their game or send them to recovery.

Trivia

Initially a commercial game, the game was later released as a freeware game on the developers' website. However, to cut on the required bandwidth, audio commentary was removed.

Description official descriptions

Take part in the Battle to save the planet Naboo, with a total of 15 land and air missions, using hovercrafts, the now famous Naboo cruiser, and all the crafts seen in the movie.

Plus, cut scenes help guide the storyline, and everyones favourite music score is thrown in to add to the atmosphere of destroying the Trade Federations plans of taking over Naboo.

A fun game!

The Good
Lots of levels, each with a new challenge and each with new vehicles to ride in. If you like Star Wars games or any games where you get to ride in Starfighters, like Star Fox, this game is a must have as the entire game takes place flying in starfighters. This game takes place during Star Wars Episode 1: The Phantom Menace. Ever wonder exactly what was happening on Naboo while Queen Amidala was on Tatooine and Coruscant? Now you'll live it, in this exciting game!

The Bad
I didn't have enough motivation to ever complete the game and I'm not sure why, but I didn't.

The Bottom Line
This game is a must-have if you enjoyed Star Fox 64, Rogue Squadron or Shadow's of the Emperor even a little bit.

Nintendo 64 · by darthsith19 (62) · 2006

Trivia

Inspiration

Loosely based on the events in Episode I: The Phantom Menace.

Graphics engine

The game uses an updated graphics engine first seen in Star Wars: Rogue Squadron 3D.

Windows port

The N64 version was ported to Windows with the additions of enhanced resolution and textures plus having an interface that allowed for mouse compatibility.

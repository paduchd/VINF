Description

You are Rallo Gump, saving the planet GUMP from Mr. Glump Gump the Third and his invading Glumpites.

Rescue your fellow citizens from their Uglybottle prisons, and save your world.

Rallo Gump is a side scrolling platform game, including features such as flying, swimming, and many hidden areas.

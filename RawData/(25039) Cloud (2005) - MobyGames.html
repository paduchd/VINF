Description

Have you ever wanted to fly amongst the clouds? In the skies above a series of small islands you will get the opportunity not only to fly amongst them but will also get a chance to manipulate them. Whether it's collecting them, redistributing them into shapes or even if you manipulate them into causing rainfall it's up to you.

This tranquil game with four levels was developed with the Game Innovation Grant from the Division of Interactive Media at the University of Southern California School of Cinema and Television. The player controls the main protagonist in his flight paths via mouse and keyboard. You are given free rein as to doing the missions in game or just free flight. Because this game was made for a school project it is offered completely free.

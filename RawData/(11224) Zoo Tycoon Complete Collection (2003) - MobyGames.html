Description official descriptions

This is a compilation of the first three Zoo Tycoon releases:

  • Zoo Tycoon (base game)
  • Zoo Tycoon: Dinosaur Digs (first expansion pack)
  • Zoo Tycoon: Marine Mania (second expansion pack)

Also included is the bonus Endangered Species Theme Pack, which features endangered animals such as orangutans, whale sharks, and Komodo dragons.

Description official descriptions

The game is based on the hit TV quiz show Who Wants to be a Millionaire? PC and Console games players can now experience for themselves the excitement and tension of the TV show as they try and win one million virtual pounds. The UK version of the game features audio clips of the TV show's host Chris Tarrant, who will be asking the questions, giving the answers, and of course, uttering the famous line, "Is that your final answer?"; other localized versions have different hosts/voices, in addition to using questions suitable for each country.

A great recreation of the TV show

The Good
It's not hard to understand why this game was a best seller: it's cheap (20$ in 2000, I got it for 8$), the system requirements are modest (you can even switch to low quality videos, or remove them completely if your PC can't take it), and it's very family friendly. It also saves high scores. I found the host (Chris Tarrant) to be quite entertaining.

The Bad
Too generic. Can't beat You don't know Jack.

The Bottom Line
Worth purchasing if you're a fan of the show and searching for a cheap thrill.

Windows · by Muresan Catalin (3) · 2008

Trivia

Milestone

Particularly notable is that this title made UK chart history by becoming the first ever title to top the All Format, PlayStation, PC and Dreamcast charts in the same week, according to an Eidos press release.

Description official descriptions

Poseidon is an expansion to Zeus: Master of Olympus, the third title in Impression's builder series. The game adds the Atlantis civilization to the mythological world of Zeus, with its own unique gods, monuments, heroes, monsters, industries and more. An adventure editor is also included to allow players to create their own scenarios.

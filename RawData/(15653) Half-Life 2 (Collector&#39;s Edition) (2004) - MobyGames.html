Description

This edition of Half-Life 2 includes the game on a limited edition DVD-ROM and some exclusive extras. It includes:

  • Half-Life 2
  • Counter-Strike: Source
  • Half-Life: Source
  • limited edition T-shirt - men's size XL only
  • Half-Life 2 Prima strategy guide

Trivia

German index

On July 30, 2005, Half-Life 2 (Collector's Edition) was put on the infamous German index by the BPjM. For more information about what this means and to see a list of games sharing the same fate, take a look here: BPjS/BPjM indexed games.

[Note: this is not because of Half-Life 2, but because of Half-Life: Source, which was put on the index on the same day.]

Description official descriptions

This release of Final Fantasy is built upon the Game Boy Advance version of the game. It retains the bonus dungeons, bestiary and unlockable BGM music player from said version, as well as the CG intro from the Playstation version. It also features improved graphics done in 16:9 format with larger fonts and higher quality of music and sound effects.

There is also an entirely new dungeon dubbed "The Labyrinth of Time" which enforces a time limit on the player, as well as an unlockable Yoshitaka Amano art gallery in this version.

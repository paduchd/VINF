Description official description

Created using Chris Jones' Adventure Game Studio, this detective non-linear story with multiple endings uses an interface very similiar to Sierra's old point & click adventure games.

In the year 2012 Jake McUrk, a 30 years old detective working for the Police Detective Agency, is put on a case that seems to be just another minor false alarm. All of sudden, Jake is thrown into a nightmare where danger lurks around every corner. During perilous investigation he finds out what exactly is going on in the city of Pleurghburg.

Very bloody, this game is not recommended for kids or even adults...

Best AGS game ever

The Good
It has an intriguing and interesting plot much like Yahtzee's 5 Days A Stranger but is longer. Pleurghburg obviously took a lot of work to make due to the massive number of endings and alternatives paths through the game.

The Bad
The graphics were the only problem. They were a bit to pixellated and the font of the subtitles made it difficult to differentiate 'O' from 'D' and 'B' from '8", etc.

The Bottom Line
Every aspect of this is good except for the graphics and the font so definitely DOWNLOAD IT NOW.

Windows · by Neon Hammerite (35) · 2005

Description

The Biohazard 5th Anniversary special package may be the ultimate collectors item for fans of the series (Resident Evil in the US). The limited edition comes packaged in a stylish aluminum suitcase. Inside the package comes with an almost-full collection of the Biohazard games:

  • Biohazard
  • Biohazard 2
  • Biohazard 3: Last Escape for the PlayStation 1
  • Biohazard Code: Veronica for the PS2 with its included Devil May Cry demo

Rounding out the collection is Wesker's Report, a region 1 coded DVD containing interviews with the series' creators.

Inside are a collection of Biohazard-themed goods including an individually numbered dog tag, a chrome pen, a keychain, and a silver ring.

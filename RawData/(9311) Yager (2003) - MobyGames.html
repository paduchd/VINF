Description official descriptions

Yager is a futuristic aerial combat game that puts the player in the role of air ace Magnus Tide. After crashing his plane, losing his job and being dumped by his girlfriend, he feels like his life can't get any worse. Fortunately, Magnus managed to get himself a new ship and a new job as a freelancer for his former employer, Proteus. Unfortunately for Magnus, his new job couldn't have come at a worse time, as the world is on the verge of full scale war.

It is this war that Magnus must survive and prosper in. From the cockpit the player must shoot through 22 missions through responsive terrain, with ground and air targets. As the game goes on, better machinery becomes available.

Yager is a gem!

The Good
Jaw-dropping graphics, immersive storyline, great flight-sim physics, plot twists, move-like cut sequences, great sound, interesting characters.

The Bad
Only two minor gripes: The inability to save game at checkpoints and the height "ceiling," most noticeable in the Bitterfeld levels. Though to be fair I never had any problems in the other levels.

The Bottom Line
In the plethora of flight-sim action games Yager stands at the top!

Windows · by Tara Taylor (11) · 2004

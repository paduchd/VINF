Description

This compilation is the successor to Gold Games 4. It offers 15 games (plus two expansion packs) from many different genres. Manuals are only available on CD in PDF format.

The following games are included:

  • Battle Isle: The Andosia War
  • Chessmaster 8000
  • Close Combat: Invasion: Normandy - Utah Beach to Cherbourg
  • Cold Blood
  • Flanker 2.0
  • Freedom: First Resistance
  • Imperialism II: The Age of Exploration
  • Prince of Persia 3D
  • Pro Rally 2001
  • Rayman 2: The Great Escape
  • Star Trek: Voyager - Elite Force
  • The Settlers III, including the first Mission CD and the second, Quest of the Amazons
  • Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six: Rogue Spear
  • Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 2
  • Warlords: Battlecry

Trivia

Release

From this volume on, the Gold Games-series is now being released by Ubi Soft Entertainment instead of Topware Interactive. That is because Topware went insolvent prior to this release.

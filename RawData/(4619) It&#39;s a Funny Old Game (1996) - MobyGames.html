Description

In this humorous soccer sim, your players are displayed as "cartoon like figures". Like many sports sims you can draft and trade players, control formations, and finances.

Trivia

Title

The title comes from a catchphrase of Jimmy Greaves, a hugely successful footballer from the 1960s, and later a successful TV pundit and wit, following several years as an alcoholic.

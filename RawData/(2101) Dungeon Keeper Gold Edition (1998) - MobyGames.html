  • a level editor

An excellent twist on a tired genre

The Good
The graphics and sounds in this game set the mood perfectly. They're always apropriate for the level. The units are unique and diversified, even if you don't directly control them. It's a unique approach to help set itself apart from your standard RTS. It's also 1 of the funniest games i've ever played.

The Bad
Your imps don't always do what u want them to. The enemy AI is a bit suspect sometimes.

The Bottom Line
A great game, if u don't own this, buy it, steal it, get it anyway you can.

Windows · by jeremy strope (159) · 2000

Trivia

Extras

There are several interesting extras in the goodies folder on the CD-ROM, including a 40mb .AVI entitled "Interview With the Horned Ripper."

The game also includes the story book The Dreadful Demise of Derek the Dauntless.

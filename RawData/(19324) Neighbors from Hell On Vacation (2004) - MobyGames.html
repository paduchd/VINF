Description

Neighbors from Hell: On Vacation is the sequel to the puzzle strategy game Neighbors From Hell. You are Woody again and have a reality show starring one annoying neighbor (again).

This time the pest has followed his unfortunate neighbor on a vacation cruise, but this time the neighbor is not alone: his mother and several other characters (such as his love interest Olga and her son) are coming with him. The game features fourteen episodes, six locations from China to Mexico, and five new characters.

Description

ClockWerx is a mind game that incorporates puzzle elements. You control a hands alike spinner and move from point to point across a field filled with obstacles. The main goal is to try to find the only one way out of the grid without hitting any obstacles, enemy spinners or other hazards on the field of play.

There are four difficulty levels and about 100 different stages with increasing number of obstacles. Obstacles consist of a wide variety of deadly items and will hinder player in any attempt to finish the game. There are also many extras such as timer points or bonus points to facilitate the completion of the level.

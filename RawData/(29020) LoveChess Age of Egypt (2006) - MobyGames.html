Description

The battle of the sexes is depicted literally as characters in costume drawn from Ancient Egypt and its rich mythology face off on a chessboard, filling the roles of its pieces and erotically dominating their adversaries when capturing an enemy piece via animations unique for every piece combination, as in Battle Chess.

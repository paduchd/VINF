Description

Portugal 1111 is an real-time strategy game with heavy influences from Age of Empires. The player can take control of either the Christian Crusaders or the Moorish Invaders as both vie for permanence in the Iberian Peninsula.

The game has only one resource - gold - which is acquired by selling wheat, or turning it into flour.

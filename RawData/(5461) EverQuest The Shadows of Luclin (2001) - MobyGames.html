Description official description

EverQuest: Shadows Of Luclin is the third expansion pack to EverQuest, the massively multiplayer role-playing game. This expansion pack adds a new world, Luclin, which is Norrath's moon. It also adds a new race, the Vah Shir, which are noble, cat-like people, and a new class, the Beastlord.

The game uses an updated graphics engine and contains many new features, including new spells and mountable horses. A new feature for high-level characters is introduced, allowing them to choose two ways of advancing; the regular, level-up way, or put points toward various character enhancements, such as dexterity and agility.

The game also re-textures the original EverQuest, The Ruins Of Kunark, and The Scars Of Velious, so that they all look alike.

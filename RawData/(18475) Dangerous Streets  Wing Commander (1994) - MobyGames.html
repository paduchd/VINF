Description

A two game compilation that came packaged with the Amiga CD32 system. It includes the 2D one-on-one fighting game Dangerous Streets, and the futuristic space-combat simulator Wing Commander (in an AGA-enhanced version only available in this compilation).

Trivia

Amiga Power reception

This disc is seen as a continuation of Commodore's tendency to pick very poor games to bundle with the Amiga lineage.The June 1994 issue of Amiga Power included a letter from frustrated CD32 owner Tom Potter, frustrated with the lack of original games for the system, but also with the bundled disc, which he dubbed the "Wing Commander / Dangerous knob, er, sorry, Streets CD". AP had a long-running joke of assigning each letter a title by plucking a string of words from the letter, often without context and ignoring sentence boundaries. This one was titled "Commander / Dangerous knob, er, sorry". One letter later joked that they dropped this phrase into dinner-party conversations at every chance they got. At least, I hope it was a joke.

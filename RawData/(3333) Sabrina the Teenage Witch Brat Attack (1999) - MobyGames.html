Description official description

This is the second in a series of game spin-offs from the popular tv show Sabrina, the teenage witch. In the game you control Sabrina on an isometric world and lead her in her quest to take back the "beanie of ultimate power" from her evil cousin Amanda. In order to defeat her you must assemple the "Anti-Brat pack" whose parts are scattered on different places around the world, and fend off enemies and monsters using a slew of different spells which come as "recipes" you must first learn and then cast by mixing the right amount of ingredients.

Description

NVA was the national army of the former German Democratic Republic aka East Germany. Based on the movie of the same name, this game is a mission-based racing title. The background story is about the young soldier Henrik who falls in love with the daughter of his captains.

There are different vehicles and environments. All of the vehicles are based on original former East German army and police crafts. Most of the missions have you race for a checkpoint, but there is also a variety of weapons, mostly rockets and mines, to deal with opponents which include police and soldiers. The game is played from a third-person perspective and there is a realistic daytime model.

Description official descriptions

Mankind is one of the first MMORTS (massively multiplayer online real-time strategy) games. You are responsible for a fleet of space ships. You start with only one but after time, you can have up to a few thousand vessels. You can also populate planets, make guilds and start wars with other players to be the master of the universe.

All access to the game is free for a limited time, but a monthly fee is required for longer play.

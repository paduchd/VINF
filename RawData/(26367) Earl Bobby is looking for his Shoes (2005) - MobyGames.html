Description

Earl Bobby is looking for his Shoes is a very short point-and-click adventure about an old Scottish earl who lives in an old castle in the Scottish highlands, and maybe also about the sense of life. It is the first part of the Earl Bobby trilogy and you will get introduced to Earl Bobby. This Earl is suffering of a kind of dementia and always loses objects. It seems to be his fate somehow.

The game starts at midnight. Earl Bobby knows that he has an important conference the next day. When he tries to dress himself he recognizes that his shoes went lost. He goes crazy and needs help. Your task is to help him in finding his shoes. You will have to solve many crazy puzzles to do so and will get to know some funny characters like Arnold the Armor and Antonio the cat.

Trivia

Inspiration

Earl Bobby is based on the Austrian joke figure Count Bobby who is still very popular in Vienna. Some movies were produced about him in the 60s.

Description official descriptions

Anstoss 2: Der FuÃŸballmanager is a soccer management simulation for 1-4 players. You are in charge of a club, participating in one of the 13 featured leagues. Start to design a training concept, implement a revolutionary tactical system and you might be on the right way. The goal is simple: reach for gold, participate in national and international competitions and win 'em all. Additionally you are responsible for the financial aspects, e.g. by building a better stadium, hiring employees, betting on other games, etc.

The matches are presented in 3D cutscenes. You can exchange players, adapt tactics to new situations or weather conditions, but don't participate directly.

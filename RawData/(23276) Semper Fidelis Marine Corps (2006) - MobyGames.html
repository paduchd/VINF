Trivia

Semper Fidelis is Latin for Always Faithful. It is no coincidence it is used for this game, as it has been the motto of The United States Marine Corps since 1883. The motto, often abbreviated as "Semper Fi", signifies the dedication that individual Marines are expected to have loyalty to "Corps and Country" and to their fellow fighting men and women, for the rest of their days and beyond. It is also the title of the corps' official march.

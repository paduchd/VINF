Trivia

Customizable content

It was possible to have your own face in the game. All you had to do was to fill in a special registration card included in the game's manual (Appendix D) with your personal details and a passport-sized photograph and send it, with a fee, to Core HQ. In return they would send you modified second disk of the game that would contain your mugshot available in the game

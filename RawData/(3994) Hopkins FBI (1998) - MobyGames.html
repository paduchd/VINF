Description official descriptions

The infamous terrorist Bernie Berckson was sentenced to death and electrocuted twice. Somehow, he remained alive, and managed to escape. Now, he's leading a new criminal organisation. As Special Agent Hopkins, you must find and arrest him and anyone belonging to his gang.

Trivia

Linux version

Hopkins FBI is known to be one of the (if not the) first commercial game for Linux.

OS/2 Warp version

This game was one of the very few to initially be published for OS/2 Warp.

Version differences

Hopkins FBI was (and still is) pretty controversial for its extremely gratuitous violence and colourful comic book depictions of grisly murders. The UK version has all the gory sequence disabled by default. They can be enabled by applying the patch available from the official site.

Description

In this game, Santa has broken his bowling ball so he will throw elves to see how far he can get. Press the mouse button to set the angle of the throw then press again to set the power. Once in the air, press the mouse button repeatedly to fly farther.

When you are on your way, you can hit balloons for bonus points, gifts to recharge your "elf-fart" flight ability and Jack-in-the-boxes that will propel you back into the air. All other items, like the polar bear, Christmas trees, clouds, penguins, and snow mounds, will drop you to the ground or bounce you backwards. Each elf adds to the total score and after three elves, you grand total is shown.

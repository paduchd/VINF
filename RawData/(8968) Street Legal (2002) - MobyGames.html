Description official descriptions

Street Legal is just like Gearhead Garage, but in Street Legal you only don't fix and build cars, you can drive with them too. Buy, Fix, Tune and Race for money or respect.

There are 4 types of cars grouped by engine types: Inline 4, V6, V8, V10. All these types have lot of customizable and upgradeable parts.

You start with 20 000$ as a rookie, then you buy yourself a car, after some tuning you are challenging club members. if you are lucky enough then you will be Pro with V10 engine car (including Turbo and NOS), lot of respect and lot of money. This is not that easy as it seems, all cars are very sentsitive about collisions. One crash with other car or touch with sidewalk, will smash your car chasis or loose something valuable like starter.

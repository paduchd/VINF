Description official descriptions

The Zeo Crystal has been shattered into shards, each representing a Zeo Rangers, who must venture into dangerous labyrinths to retrieve them. However, the villainous Machine Empire will do everything in its power to stop the Rangers. Will the super-heroes be able to defeat its minions?..

Power Rangers Zeo Versus The Machine Empire is an action platformer based on the Power Rangers television show. Each level is dedicated to a particular crystal. The player controls several Zeo Rangers, depending on the stage and crystal type. The main objective in every stage is to gather crystal shards of the corresponding color. Naturally, the Rangers have to deal with the machines sent by the Empire. The heroes also have to avoid hazards, jump, shimmy on poles, and perform other characteristic platformer activities.

Description official descriptions

10six Online is an on-line only action/adventure game, meant to become the world's first 1 million player game. It runs 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, even when you're not in the game, so you better not let your defenses down.

The game is based on a planet in the galaxy called Visitor. There is a metal on the planet that can only be gotten there, called Transnium (TU). The object of the game is to sell as much TU as possible. You have to join a mining corperation on the planet, then claim a plot of land (camp).

Sound easy? Well, it's a bit harder then that for there are many other people on the planet that want your camp and its TU. So you'll have to build turrets and rovers to help defend your investment from all the other people on the planet, and join an MDN.

The more camps you build, the more money you get for selling your TU, and the more there is to defend. But you have to sell TU to make money, and the more money you have, the more powerful you will become. Also, you aren't the only person with rovers and many camps, many other people also have a lot of camps.

The game takes place in a fully 3-d environment, in real time, and uses advanced AI on all the turrets and rovers.

Shutdown

SEGA shut down 10six on 31st May, 2002. The company offered a refund for anyone with remaining subscription time that would be cut short.

Description official descriptions

Dead or Alive Ultimate features the original SEGA Saturn version of Dead or Alive as well as a totally redesigned Dead or Alive 2 along with more costumes and more features including the addition of Live! support so the player can take their fight online.

A awesome fighting game and a spectacular remake of DOA2.

The Good
The graphics are amazing. I never seen such detail into the characters in a xbox. The roster of fighters are awesome, that's the best part of the game. the interactive battle grounds are very cool, you can knock a enemy flying into a elephant!

The Bad
I found it strange that Tecmo created a physics engine just for the female character's breasts. and why do they have revealing suits?

The Bottom Line
Just go the store and buy it, i got the last copy at wal-mart. It's just a spectacular fighter with the best graphics on the Xbox.

Xbox · by JohnLennon224 (13) · 2007

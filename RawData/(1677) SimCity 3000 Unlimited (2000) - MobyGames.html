Description official descriptions

SimCity 3000 Unlimited is a re-release of the original SimCity 3000, which adds additional features such as 2 new building sets (European and Asian), new scenarios, new disasters, a scenario creator, seasonal city events and the Building Architect Tool, which was previously a downloadable add-on for the base game.

SimCity 3000: Deutschland and SimCity 3000: UK Edition change the European set to have a more national look.

Best SimCity yet!

The Good
I loved this game. It resembles a real city in so many ways. The people living in your cities will react to just about anything you do. I loved the ability to meet with other people in your city. They might be complaining, complimenting, or giving you stuff for being a good mayor. You can zoom in to the point where you can see people walking on the street. I even say a zombie walking around! Everything in this SimCity is way better than in SimCity 2000, including the music. It is so good, I wish there was a soundtrack to go with the game.

The Bad
I guess I really can't think of anything bad about this game.

The Bottom Line
A really good game for anyone who likes strategy. An even better game if you love the SimCity series. I've never played SimCity 3000 before, only this one, so I really don't know what the differences are between them. I know that the gameplay is pretty much the same, so I can say that SimCity 3000 Unlimited is a big step from SimCity 2000. Everything is well marked, so you don't really need any time in the manual to figure out how it works. All in all, an excellent game by me.

Windows · by Mullet of Death (592) · 2005

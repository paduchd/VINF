Description

One of the Pack 5 compilations from Global Star Software, this bargain priced package contains full versions of:

  • Bejeweled: The addictive jewel arranging puzzle game.
  • Moraff's Maximum Mahjongg: 4 variations of the classic tile matching puzzle game.
  • King Sol Solitaire: 400 different solitaire card games.
  • Pro Series Chess: You guessed it, a chess game.
  • Assimilation: A marble and board territory taking puzzle game.

Trivia

As is often the case with compilations, what you see isn't always what you get. In this case, there's a bonus game. Instead of just Bejeweled, it contains the Bejeweled/Alchemy double pack.

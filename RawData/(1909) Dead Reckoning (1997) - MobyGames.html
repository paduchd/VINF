Description official descriptions

In Dead Reckoning you are abducted by aliens known only as the "Master Race" and forced to fight for your life. Pick your ship and wingmen and get ready to do battle against alien races in cylinder shaped battlefields. Just one thing, if you lose the human race gets obliterated.

Description official description

True Pinball is a console version of Pinball Illusions that has 4 different tables, named "Extreme Sports", "Vikings - The Tales", "Ricochet" and "Babewatch".

Both 2D and 3D views (not available in computer versions) are available and over 32,000 colors in high resolutions boost it visually.

Excellent pinball game!

The Good
The graphics are great The sounds are nice. Controls are pretty good. Low load time. If you get a high score and either have save space in the system or on a memory card, you can enter your initials.

The Bad
It has only has four pinball machines to choose from and only gives you 30 seconds to enter your initials for a high score.

The Bottom Line
This is great game for anyone who likes pinball. Very realistic. Graphics are comparable to Dreamcast in gameplay. Basic control of the flippers are easy to do. You can even shake the pinball machine (but be careful not to TILT it!). For a rating I gave all 5's except personal slant due to there only being 4 pinball machines to choose from. After playing it a lot, there is only so much entertainment left. Aside from that it's great!

SEGA Saturn · by Teddy Ruxpin (144) · 2005

Trivia

This game contains the same pinball tables as Pinball Illusions, also by Digital Illusions. Only this time the tables are modeled in 3D.

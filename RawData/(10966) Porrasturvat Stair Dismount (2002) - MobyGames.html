Description

Stair dismount is the predecessor to Truck dismount. The legendary superhero Spector has found, to his shock, that he cannot write off all the damage he has caused to the city out of his taxes unless he proves that he has sustained significant damage in the process himself.

You come to the rescue. Spector is placed on top of some stairs and you select which body part to push and the angle. Time your click on the push button when the pulsating force bar reaches the desired level. Points are awarded through the sustained damage. The game features many camera positions and a highscore list.

Original and fun

The Good
It is a lot of fun to push someone off the top of some stairs and listen to his squeals of pain as he tumbles down. There are a lot of options so you can choose how hard to push, where on his body to push and what angle to push from.

The Bad
It is not a realistic looking game, the character and the stairs are made mainly of simple shapes.

The Bottom Line
Sick, twisted, never seen anything like it before.

Windows · by Jeremy Petho (245) · 2006

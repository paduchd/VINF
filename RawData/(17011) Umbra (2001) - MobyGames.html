Description official description

In the quaint Long Island town of Port Washington, everything seems perfectly fine as it always is. That is, until a mysterious solar eclipse causes a local school to mysteriously vanish, as well as the teacher trapped inside. You, a student at the vanished school, begins with a simple clue, and must find the answer to the mysterious events.

Umbra's graphics are entirely made up of photos taken in Port Washington, New York. The game has about ten different possible stories that change every time you play, giving it a total of over 30 hours of replay value.

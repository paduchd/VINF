Description official descriptions

Based on the famous movie series of the same name about good boxer Rocky Balboa (played by Sylvester Stallone).

The boxing simulator game has several modes, including Movie Mode, Exhibition and Knockout Tournament. In Movie Mode you can follow the movie series, starting each part with three minor opponents and finishing the big boss in the end. Each character has personal weaknesses. Also you can train Rocky prior to each fight.

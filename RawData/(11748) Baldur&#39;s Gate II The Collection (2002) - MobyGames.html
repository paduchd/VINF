Description official descriptions

This compilation contains:

  • Baldur's Gate II: Shadows of Amn (base game)
  • Baldur's Gate II: Throne of Bhaal (expansion pack)
  • a bonus CD containing additional weapons and armor and a set of four character portraits

Trivia

Soundtrack

Interplay had originally planned on publishing the soundtrack along with the game, but a mistake from their part caused the Baldur's Gate II: The Collection to ship without it.

As a result, Interplay offered the complete soundtrack as a Download from their website.

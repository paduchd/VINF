Description official description

This "edutainment" product enables the user to create real 3D models of any structure (homes, office buildings, movie theaters, shops, schools, missions, etc.). Models may be created from scratch or edited from templates provided. Hometown U.S.A. was released for the PC(DOS), Macintosh, Apple II, Amiga, and Commodore 64.

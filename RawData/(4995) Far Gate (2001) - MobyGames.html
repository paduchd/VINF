Description official descriptions

Far Gate is a real time strategy game that takes place in space. After finding what seems to be another inhabitable planet not far from the Solar System, colonists are sent to the new planet, only to find that it isn't inhabitable at all. What they do find is two new races, completely different from human beings:

  • The Nue-Guyen: An organic based species whose units are living entities.
  • The Entrodii: Crystalline based life forms that see no use for organics.

Both are out to destroy the human race from the galaxy. And factions on Earth are starting to cause major problems in addition.

It is up to you and your command fleet to protect Earth from the many enemies, both human and non-human, using all of the technologically advanced space ships available to you.

Trivia

Inspiration

During an interview back in 2017, Jeff House stated some inspirations for Far Gate. The group wanted it to have a feel akin to Space Battleship Yamato, which shows with the way the character portraits were drawn. The reason they also went in the direction of creatures/entities as the enemies was because of the release of Homeworld, which they felt they'd copy too much if they had alien ships.

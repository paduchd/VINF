Description official descriptions

A first person shooter that requires you to think things through rather than just attack anything that moves. This game preceded Wolfenstein 3D by a number of years, and while the walls were not ray cast, but solid fill polygons, it was still one of the earliest simulations to tackle a human viewpoint with complete, 360 degree freedom of movement.

Trivia

Customizable content

It was possible to have your own face in the game. All you had to do was to fill in a special registration card included in the game's manual (Appendix D) with your personal details and a passport-sized photograph and send it, with a fee, to Core HQ. In return they would send you modified second disk of the game that would contain your mugshot available in the game

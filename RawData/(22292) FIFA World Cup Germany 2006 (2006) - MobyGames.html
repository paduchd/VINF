Description official descriptions

Released one month before the actual World Cup 2006, this game lets players play all the matches before they even started. Featuring 12 official German stadiums and all the teams that qualified for the World Cup 2006 in Germany, plus additional teams and unlockable classic players. The playable modes let you try any of the qualifying stages or skip right to the world cup mode assuming as one of the qualified teams. Additional modes include Global Challenge where you can replay 40 historical moments in football history to change the outcome and various multiplayer matches.

Trivia

Cover

The cover design has been localized with different athletes portrayed for specific release countries:* England: Frank Lampard (England) * China: Zhi Zhen (China) * Germany: Lukas Podolski (Germany) * Italy: Alessandro Del Piero (Italy) * USA, Canada and Mexico: Jamie Lozano (Mexico) and Claudio Reyna (USA) * Portugal: Simao (Portugal) * The Netherlands, Belgium and Luxemburg: Arjen Robben (The Netherlands) * Switzerland: Ricardo Cabanas (Switzerland) * Spain: Xavi (Spain) * All other countries: the FIFA World Cup trophy and the official emblem

Flag inaccuracy

The country Serbia and Montenegro ceased to exist on 3 June 2006, when Montenegro voted for independence in a referendum. However, since the 2006 FIFA World Cup was scheduled to start just six days later, it was decided that the national selection of Serbia and Montenegro remained as it was for the duration of the competition. However, on the cover art variants of this licensed game which show multiple flags, the flag of separate Serbia is used instead of the accurate Serbia and Montenegro flag.

Online servers

On 1st September 2007, EA closed their servers for multiplayer games, for the Xbox, Windows, PS2 and PSP versions.

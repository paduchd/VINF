Description

Team Factor is an online team-based FPS (First Person Shooter) in which the player can take control of one of the four characters (Sniper, Soldier, Scout & Weapons Expert) in one of the three groups (Terrorists, US Special Forces & Russian Specnaz) through the 22 maps, each with its own objectives.

Team Factor uses a RPG system that basically allows the player to choose and develop their own unique way to fight by setting up whichever skills they likes to work on. Later on, players will be granted with access to better equipment according to the skills developed. The player's character info is being stored in an online database on the server.

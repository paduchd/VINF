Description

This compilation is a re-packaging of the first six volumes of the compilation series with the same name, Best of Sierra.

Not just the games, but each complete issue in the way it was sold in the first place. So it contains all six magazines with manuals, walkthroughs, articles, interviews, etc. and all six CD's with demos, trailers, etc.

This is inside the box:

  • Best of Sierra Nr. 1:

    • Gabriel Knight: Sins of the Fathers
    • Metaltech: Earthsiege

  • Best of Sierra Nr. 2:

    • King's Quest VII: The Princeless Bride
    • The Even More! Incredible Machine
    • King's Quest: Quest For The Crown
  • Best of Sierra Nr. 3:
    • 3-D Ultra Pinball: Creep Night
    • Freddy Pharkas: Frontier Pharmacist
  • Best of Sierra Nr. 4:
    • Space Quest VI: Roger Wilco in the Spinal Frontier
    • Lode Runner: The Legend Returns
  • Best of Sierra Nr. 5:
    • Woodruff and The Schnibble of Azimuth
    • Caesar
  • Best of Sierra Nr. 6:
    • Hunter Hunted
    • Aces Over Europe

Trivia

Title

Either the compilation should be called Best of Sierra - 13 Top Games or King's Quest is not a Top Game.

Why? The Top Games relates to the title games of each issue. There are two title games in each issue and six issues in this box, all in all are 12 games. But issue 2 actually contains three games, besides the two title games, there is also a bonus game included, King's Quest. So there are actually 13 games in the box.

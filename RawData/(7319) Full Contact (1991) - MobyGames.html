Description official description

A platform fighting game that you can play in several different ways. You can fight against your friend or a foe on the same computer, using two joysticks, or joystick + keyboard combination. You can play tournament, or as a solo, fighting through the serious of computer AI controlled fighters (even a dog), with one tougher then the other. The reward is as usual, being top on the top 10 list.

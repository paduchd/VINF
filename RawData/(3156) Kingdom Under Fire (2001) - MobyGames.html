Description official description

Evil looms large in the land of Bersiah. The last time good battled evil, over 100 years ago, good came out strong. But this time, evil is out for revenge, and is stronger than ever. What you must decide is who to fight with, as there are two campaigns to choose from, either good or evil. Which ever you do choose, you will lead into battle, with many different races to work with (including Knights, Orcs, Dragons and Wizards galore) in 10 strategic battles, as well as three dungeon based levels (much like Diablo). KUF also includes plenty of voice acting, lighting effects much like the Baldur's Gate series, and combines RTS with RPG elements.

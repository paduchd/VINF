Description official descriptions

Sprung is a dating game that takes place at an upscale ski resort.

Play as either Brett or Becky, two young and attractive adults, on vacation - each "looking for love". Each character has a separate story with entirely different escapades.

They'll interact with scantily clad babes and hunks in this "simulation" of the dating scene. Their adventures take place all over town: at the ski lodge, in a gondola, at a popular nightclub, and, of course, in a hot tub.

The game features humorous dialogs and adult situations with upbeat music and 2D graphics.

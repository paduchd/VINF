Description official description

Might qualify as one of the strangest games of all time. Needs to be played to be understood. Features various arcade games and you playing as Baba Booey.

Trivia

Controversy

According to Mark's Friggin' website, the owners of the Howard Stern show don't take kindly to parodies. Friggin was forced to take down his "adventure" series of games.

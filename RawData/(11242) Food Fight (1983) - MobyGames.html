Description official description

You play as Charley trying to eat an ice cream cone. But first you must avoid the chefs that are out to get you: Angelo, Zorba, Oscar, and Jacques. Avoid the food thrown at you by the chefs to get the cone. You can also throw your own food at the chefs to slow them down.

Average score: 3.2 out of 5 (based on 12 ratings with 1 reviews)

Throw food, Avoid thrown food. Run around.

The Good
This was a strange little arcade game when it came out. And it wasn't available on a home console until the 7800 version was available. It's utterly simple but extremely fun at the same time. You get to throw food! Making a mess is a good thing.

The Bad
The controls are a bit hard to get used to. The arcade version was nice and smooth. The 7800 version seemed stuck in 8 direction only. Other than that I have no complaints.

The Bottom Line
You start your guy on one side of the screen. On the other side is your goal, a dripping Ice Cream Cone. You must eat it before the last drip. Between you and your goal are a few angry Chefs, some piles of food and some holes. Stay away from the holes of course unless you time it right when they pop shut. When you are standing on top of food you can push your button and throw it at the chefs. This slows them down. If they touch you, its over. If you get hit with food, its over. Fall down a hole, you guessed it, its over. Just get yourself close to that goal and it's on to the next level. The simple act of tossing the food or watching yourself get splattered with all the food on the level when you get hit is a pleasure. Because of the limited release of this game it is one of the "Must Have" 7800 titles.

Atari 7800 · by gametrader (208) · 2006

Trivia

Cancelled Atari ST version

A conversion to the Atari ST was made by Paradox for Atari, but was never finished. The unfinished game has been leaked and can be viewed at Atari Legend.

Launch game

Food Fight was one of the "Fabulous Eleven" launch games for the Atari 7800.

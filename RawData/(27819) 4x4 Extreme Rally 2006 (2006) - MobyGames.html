Description official description

4x4 Extreme Rally 2006 is a top-down rally with 27 tracks all over the world. There are 4 cars to choose from and they can be upgraded in different ways after winning races. The available game modes are Time Trial, Race, Cup and 2-player multiplayer in the multiYou! Bluetooth mode. The different tracks need to be unlocked by winning races. Each Cup or Race game is played with 3 computer-controlled opponents. The tracks contain obstacles and different surfaces that slow down the vehicles, but there are also shortcuts to drift through and different weather schemes. Prior to each race, there are 5 music tracks to choose from.

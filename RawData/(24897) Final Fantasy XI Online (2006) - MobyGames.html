Description

This collection is an updated release of Final Fantasy XI Online: The Vana'Diel Collection. In addition to the previous compilation with the original game and two expansion packs, it now includes three add-ons:* Rise of the Zilart

  • Chains of Promathia
  • Treasures of Aht Urhgan

The collection comes with a fold-out map of Vana'diel with mini walkthroughs to help new players get started, a 30-day free trial, and a Vana'diel buddy pass to invite a friend to try the game free for 30 days. It is released at a budget price.

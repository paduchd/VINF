Description

A non-graphic, detailed simulation of owning and managing an NFL franchise, including roster decisions, financial decisions, and in-game playcalling.

Like other managerial sports games, FOF offers realistic financial rules and models. Salary caps, trading and drafting rules, and collective bargaining requirements are all faithfully modelled. Even blackmailing a city to buy you a new stadium is modelled.

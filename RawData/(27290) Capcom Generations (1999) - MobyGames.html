Description

This is a compilation of the 4 parts of Capcom Generation series of games, bundled up together in a 4-disc release for the European market. Included are:

  • Disc 1 - "Wings Of Destiny": 1942, 1943 and 1943 Kai.
  • Disc 2 - "Chronicles Of Arthur": Ghosts 'n Goblins, Ghouls 'n Ghosts and Super Ghouls 'n Ghosts.
  • Disc 3 - "The First Generation": Vulgus, Son Son, Pirate Ship Higemaru and Exed Exes.
  • Disc 4 - "Blazing Guns": Commando, Mercs and Gun.Smoke.

As for extras, there is a Collection on each disc with information about the games and some illustrations. Several of the games also feature a "remix" feature for the music if you want to play the games with a slightly more modern sound scape in the background than the rather simple melodies that were on the games originally.

Trivia

German release

The German version of the game omits the fourth disc, as two of the games present on said disc (Commando and Gun.Smoke) were still part of the infamous German BPjS/BPjM index, meaning that Capcom Generations would've had the same fate had those games been left in.

Releases

Disk one and two were released as individual games. The price for each other is near to the complete collection.

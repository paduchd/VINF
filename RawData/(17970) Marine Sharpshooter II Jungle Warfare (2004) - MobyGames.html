Description official description

This sequel to CTU: Marine Sharpshooter places a player-controlled sniper team in the African nation of Burundi at the potential dawn of a civil war. Rebels have kidnapped President Ndayizeze and it's up to the player to find and rescue him.

Jungle warfare uses the same shooter/spotter system as the first game with an emphasis on using cover and stealth to avoid detection and make long range kills. There are ten missions to complete using three different rifles and a variety of high tech gear.

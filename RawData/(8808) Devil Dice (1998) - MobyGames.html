Description official description

Devil Dice is a multi-player puzzle game. Use devils to wipe out rows of dice, play one-to-one challenges or take on four friends in a frenetic battle for survival.

There is a choice of four different game modes: race against the clock, attempt increasingly challenging puzzles, team up with a friend or battle to be the sole survivor.

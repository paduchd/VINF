Availability

There are only 3,000 copies in the world. The game comes with a Scroll of Authentication that reads:

*Certificate of Authentication

The Companions of the Lance Hereby bestow upon you the right of ownership for the Advanced Dungeons and Dragons Limited Edition Collectors Pack[sic]Number ___ of 3000*

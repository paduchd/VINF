Description

This free disc contains add-on items of a portal to the Dark Isle, a skeleton key that opens every door in the Free Tour mode, a white orb and a black orb that can be used in the full game of Of Light and Darkness: The Prophecy. It also contains a non-interactive video of the game as well as a Windows 95 theme.

Trivia

This disk was a free promotional item with a magazine.

Description official description

2x includes both Tony Hawk 1 and 2, together with improvements to backgrounds and player models, new Treyarch levels exclusive to the Xbox, and the ability to use all your favorite Tony Hawk 2 tricks in the original Tony Hawk. Plus between 4 and 8 players can play simultaneously in the many multiplayer options including Trick Attack, Graffiti, Tag, HORSE and Free Skate.

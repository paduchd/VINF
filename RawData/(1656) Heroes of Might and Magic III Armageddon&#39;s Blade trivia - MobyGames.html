Trivia

Forge Town

During the development phase of Armageddon's Blade, 3DO unveiled a ninth town - the Forge Town, which was to bring high technology and a futuristic setting to the game. However, the fans panned this move due to the "distastefulness" of the town (especially some concepts, like the bare-breasted Naga Tank). 3DO employees also received death threats via mail, so they scrapped the Forge Town in favour of the Conflux.

References

The cheat codes in this expansion are all references to Star Wars Episode I, NwcDarthMaul for example.

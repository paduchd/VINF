Description official descriptions

The Sims: House Party is an add-on pack for The Sims and is all about, well, parties. Everything in it is designed to make parties more fun for both you and your Sims. There are over 100 new objects and characters; 5 new music styles; 5 new dance styles to match the new music; and three new themes -- Cowboy, Luau, and Rave.

Featuring over 100 new party favors and essentials! Take a ride on the Mechanical Bull, dress the occasion and enjoy the costume trunk, test your dance skills in the Dance Cage, add some pop to your party with the Bubble Maker, plus a Campfire for your outdoor gigs! House Party also includes new chacacters to the fun: resident DJs, Psycho Mime, Campfire Ghost, Entertainer, Caterer, plus special guests. Choose from Rap, Techo, Country, Beach and Disco music spinning. Your Sims can enjoy 3 distinct party styles: Rave, Cowboy and Luau.

For Sims diehards, largely...

The Good
The Sims House Party adds a variety of new objects, furniture, and group interactions to a great game!

The Bad
...but for the price, it doesn't really add enough.

The Bottom Line
Compared to other Sims expansions that really did amazing things for the game, like Livin' Large and Hot Date, House Party isn't really all that impressive. There are certain internet downloads that require that HP be installed to work, though, so if you're really going to get SERIOUS about sims, go ahead and buy it...

Windows · by Dr.Bedlam (55) · 2002

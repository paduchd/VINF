Description

In this game the player takes the role of a detective trying to compile enough evidence to get Paul Robinson arrested. The game is set in Ramsey Street where the Australian soap opera of the same name is set.

The game is a first person point-and-click adventure which uses stills from the actual program and allows players to interact with various characters from the show. Moving around the street involves clicking on the direction on the compass. Interacting with people and objects is done by clicking on the relevant button.

Trivia

This game somewhat returns to significance at present, as Paul Robinson (played by Stefan Dennis) is currently back in the programme, one of many former stars returning for the show's 20th anniversary. Having done time in jail, he's gone to a lot of effort to paint himself as a community minded figure who can be trusted, but he's already burnt down Lassiter's (and murdered Gus in the process), slept with Izzy, made a move for Liljana, fraudulently got David elected to local government, attempted to frame him for an unconnected fraud (involving a charity named after his late Grandmother, original character Helen Daniels), and used him as a pawn to get his shady business dealings in place.

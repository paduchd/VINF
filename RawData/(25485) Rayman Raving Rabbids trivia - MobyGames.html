1001 Video Games

The Wii version of Rayman Raving Rabbids appears in the book 1001 Video Games You Must Play Before You Die by General Editor Tony Mott.

Unicef

The rabbids from this game were used in three commercial for Unicef, promoting nutrition, education and the freedom of expression for everyone. Links to the videos can be found in the related links section.

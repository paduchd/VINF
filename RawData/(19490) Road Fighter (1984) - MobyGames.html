Description

A top view racing game. You have to reach the finish line before time runs out. Don't crash into the wall, cars or trucks. Because if you do you will lose gasoline. You can get additional gasoline by picking up fuel items. Especially avoid the purple cars, they do change lanes! And remember... oil makes road slippery...

Trivia

Easter Eggs

If you manage to stay on the road without any collisions for a certain amount of time, the Konami Man will appear flying by the side of the road! If he shows up, the player is awarded a 1,000 point bonus in the NES/Famicom version and 3,000 points in the Arcade. Interestingly, this is the first appearance of the Konami Man character in a Konami game.

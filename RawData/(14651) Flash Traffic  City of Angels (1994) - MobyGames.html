Description official description

A DEA team raids an apartment in the City of Angels. Among the requisite drugs and crazed loonies, the team discovers something far more chilling: "The place was used to build a nuclear bomb," to quote your FBI sidekick, Sawyer. As if that isn't bad enough, the bomb is gone and in the hands of a crazy German terrorist who is planning to set it off somewhere in the city. And considering your first lead is the terrorist's ex-girlfriend, stopping him isn't going to be easy.

And so you're off, watching FMV sequences and occasionally deciding between three dialogue choices in situations ranging from orders to SWAT teams to interrogating some of the terrorists' bomb-making cronies.

Trivia

Development

Game writer Chuck Pfarrer has also written several Hollywood screenplays, including those for The Jackal (1997) and Red Planet (2000).

Version differences

There was an alternate version of this game released which required the use of a RealMagic Interactive MPEG Playback Controller by Sigma Designs, which was basically an MPEG Decoder Card.

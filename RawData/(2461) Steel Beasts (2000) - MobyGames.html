Description official descriptions

A tank simulation that takes place in the present age. You fight in Kuwait, Europe, Korea and other locales.There are two tanks the player can control, playing from the gunner or tank commander's position: America's M1A1 Abrams and Germany's Leopard 2A4.

Hard-Core Realism

The Good
The realism is awesome. I'm a tanker myself, and this game is the closest you'll ever get to real life tanking, unless you join the army. The missions are great, and the options you have for controlling your units in the battle are outstanding. You control the entire battle plan, from artillery strikes to small unit tactics. The creators of this game are all officers in tank units, and this is certainly reflected in the game. Also, the support you get from the creators is outstanding. Post your question on SSnakes website, and you'll have the full answer within 24 hours.

The Bad
The absence of a campaign is a downside, although I realise the difficulty in creating a credible campaign. I would also like to be able to control more vehicles, although I usually find myself spending more time on the map, and concerning my self with the overall tactical situation, than in the tank.

The Bottom Line
If you like realism and tanks/warfare. get this game. If you haven't got much experience with tank simulators, you'll have the opprtunity to go through tutorials to learn the basics of tanking. Buy this game.

(www.steelbeasts.com)

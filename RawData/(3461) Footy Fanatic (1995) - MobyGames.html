Description

You have just been accepted manager in a new European Super League to a team of your choice. There are over a hundred different teams to manage from all over Europe, and it is fun to take teams from minnow countries to the Serie A.

Reviews

Players

Average score: 3.2 out of 5 (based on 4 ratings with 1 reviews)

THE best Shareware/Freeware football management game ever!

The Good
Everything. Lots of options, very user friendly. The results play to your strategies, and you can even create your own scenarios and take your Sunday team to the European Championship. The players are real, the stadiums are changeable, and you can even choose what to practice. Very good.

The Bad
There are no graphics, and you can only play 35 seasons (but you retire after that, so it's still realistic). Other than that, Championship Manager style.

The Bottom Line
Download this game NOW. This is the most underrated football sim in the world.

DOS · by Diamond Titan (66) · 2002

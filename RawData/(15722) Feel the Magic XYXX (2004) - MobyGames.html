Description official descriptions

A game in which you "The Hero" must succeed in attracting the girl of your dreams by performing insane stunts.

The stunts are carried out in some 30 mini games involving the touch screen and the microphone. The games are fast and have ten levels of difficulty. Touch screen games include such objectives as pushing goldfish out of a mans stomach, riding a unicycle over a zigzag of boards between two sky scrappers, and so on. Mic games include blowing to create various wind strength to sail a boat, and yelling to get the attention of your beloved. Longer "Boss" games also appear. Once a game is completed in the story mode it will be available in a section called "Memories". This mode will allow you to play the games in all their difficulty with one life to earn stars. Stars then used to unlock optional costumes for the dream girl.

The game has both English and Japanese language support.

Trivia

Inspiration

Feel the Magic XY/XX creator Takumi Yoshinaga explained his motivation at a press event.

I create my games with the hope they will make people happy when they play it...I'd like to keep creating these kinds of titles, which will leave something [in the hearts] of the players. The [key] point is communion between the heart of people and people.

References

You can unlock hairstyles from various Sonic Team games such as Sonic, Space Channel 5 and Nights, by starting the game with a Sonic Team game in the GBA slot.

Title translation

The Japanese title for this game, Kimi no tame nara shineru, translates to "I could die for you".

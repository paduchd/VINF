Description

The little hero Murong Qingfeng is a rebellious character, who often goes against the tradition. His parents died when he was a baby, and his sisters and brothers could never understand him. He left his home and lived alone in the mountains. After ten years of solitary life, he practiced kung-fu, hoping to become a great adventurer. And when one day he met a mysterious person, a master of xugu (a kind of kung-fu), he understood that this is his chance to help this person to revive the disappearing magic, and to become the most famous martial artists in China.

Jian zhi Hun is a Chinese RPG, set in old China and influenced by popular legends of kung-fu masters and their supernatural powers. You navigate your hero from third-person perspective over pre-rendered backgrounds. Enemies are visible on screen, and a contact with an enemy triggers a turn-based battle, which also uses a third-person view. You can attack physically or use one of the many kung-fu techniques you learn.

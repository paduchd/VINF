Description

This freeware, fan-made sequel to Spear of Destiny comes with all-new sprites, weapons, and exploding barrels.

Want to find out what really happened to Adolf Hitler after the war? Why his body was never recovered? What did the Nazis do after escaping persecution? Who got hold of the so-called powerful mystical Spear of Destiny? This game boasts 23 levels including secret ones.

In 2011 the game was ported to Windows using SDL, allowing it to run natively on modern PCs using a wide range of higher resolution graphics. To mark the game's 10th anniversary, this conversion was accompanied by an all-new map-set for extra challenge, though the original 2001 maps can still be used.

Trivia

Engine

Though it appears to include a plethora of bonus interface ornaments and functionality that the original Spear of Destiny games lacked (such as exploding barrels and outdoor weather effects), it would be a mistake to assume that this game was based on the more advanced DOOM engine. All of these extra effects were in fact achieved by directly wrestling with the original Spear of Destiny source code, released by iD in 1999.

Game Soundtrack

The music that can be heard in the first level is actually a digitized version of a song composed by director John Carpenter for his 1976 movie "Assault on Precinct 13". Information contributed by Pseudo_Intellectual and JudgeDeadd

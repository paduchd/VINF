Description official descriptions

Larry Laffer, the protagonist of the Leisure Suit Larry adventure series, is running a casino. Leisure Suit Larry's Casino offers 5 casino games, 3 party games, a full gift shop, 3 restaurants, 3 bars, and a few surprises.

Trivia

Larry 8

When you click on the in-game credits, there is advertisement indicating the soon to be released title Larry 8: Lust in Space. The game was never completed or released.

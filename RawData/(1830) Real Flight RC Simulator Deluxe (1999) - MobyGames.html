Description official descriptions

This handy Sim allows you to Crash and bash your model planes to your hearts content. A very handy learning tool to those who actually wish to fly R/C (remote control) planes. It comes with a neat controller that plugs into your gaming port (usually soundcard). This controller is looks and feels like the actual radio controller you use in "real life". There are 6 feilds you can fly at with ten planes and 10 helicopters, both of which can be edited to resemble your own plane (eg. different motor). Wind speed, vector, and constance can be altered with a couple klicks of the mouse.

Description

You have become separated from your fellow soldiers. You thought the men approaching you were your rescue but they were the enemy. You must make your way through to return to your friends.

This is a side-scrolling platform game where you walk along, avoiding danger. When you encounter an enemy, your only attack is a kick so plan your strategy accordingly. Be careful with enemies that can shoot and when you are on dangerous ledges.

Trivia

Milestone

This was the last game produced under the company name Color Dreams before the company name changed to Wisdom Tree.

Original planned license

At one time, this game was going to be a Six Million Dollar Man game featuring Lee Majors. The idea was dropped, most likely to save on licensing the name and likeness.

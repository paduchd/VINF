Description official description

Motocross Madness 2 is a motocross simulation. It can be played in many different ways. There are stunt tracks, where you have to perform the craziest stunts to earn points; supercross tracks, where you have to pass the finish line before the others do; baja tracks, where you have to ride a circuit through the wilderness; and new enduro tracks, where you also have to ride through outdoor locations, but each with a special theme. There are a lot of obstacles that make the scenery very realistic. With the downloadable editor called "Armadillo", you can build your own tracks and objects.

A truly amazing game

The Good
The landscapes are huge with plenty of hills and mountains for you to ride around on, and thousands of objects to make the landscape look amazing.

And, you can download an official track editor off their website, which makes it really easy to create your own tracks aswell. There are lots of fan websites as well, with tons of add-ons.

Also, the way you control your bike is great. You press the arrow keys to tip your bike, and use another key for accelerate. This means that when you are in mid-air, you can tip yourself however you want to ensure the perfect landing.

The Bad
The texturing could have been a bit better, and the AI is really bad - I put it on the hardest difficulty and beat it easily, and it looks really jolty and unrealistic.

The Bottom Line
This is an amazing game, with lots of detail on the landscapes. A great game for motocross fans.

Windows · by James Isaac (239) · 2004

Good for a laugh.

The Good
There are a wide variety of tracks and a good selection of bikes available. The pace of the game is fast and furious. Sounds are generally good and graphics are ok. The game is available for under five pounds! There is a huge community on the web with everything from new bike skins to new tracks. Its a fun game.

The Bad
The sound when you crash follows the bike and not the rider, so the bike grunts and groans! The graphics could be much better.

The Bottom Line
A very cheap (price) fun game for those of you with a dirty soul and a lust for a blast round a moto track,

Windows · by sbikerbud (9) · 2003

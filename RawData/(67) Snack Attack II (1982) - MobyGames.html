Description official description

Like the first Snack Attack game, the sequel is a gluttony-rewarding revamp of Pac-Man starring Snacker the whale. Snacker is having another bad case of the munchies, and decides to explore the mazes for delicious gumdrops to gobble up. As before, he'll have to evade the Gumdrop Guards who seek to put a stop to his candy binge; each maze contains a number of magic stars that let him dine on the guards for a limited time, and special doorways that admit either Snacker or the guards, but not both. Clear all three different mazes, and they'll repeat at a higher speed with tougher guards.

This time, the game is further enhanced by new maze layouts, six difficulty levels, and enemies that vary in behavior and speed; the bonus foodstuff that sometimes appears gives Snacker a temporary speed-boost, too. The top score for each skill level is recorded on disk.

Description official descriptions

Profitania is an underground kingdom inhabited by exotic creatures known for their ingenuity. Recently, the Profitanians made an amazing discovery. They learned how to harness the giant magma pools near the planet's core and use this energy to power their emerging industry. As a budding entrepreneur, you have constructed a new factory and hope to take advantage of this economic boom. You must compete against five other players in a race to become the leading underground industrialist. The first company to dominate the market by reaching a cash value of one million gems wins the game!

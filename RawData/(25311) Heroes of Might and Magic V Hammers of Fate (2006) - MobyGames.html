Description official descriptions

In the first campaign of three new ones in this expansion pack for Heroes of Might and Magic V, you control Freyda as she struggles with a civil war in the Griffin Empire. After that, you move to the icy mountains in the north, which the dwarves call their home.

They are the new playable race in Hammers of Fate and bring along a new kind of city called Fortress. Since dwarves do not know anything about mana and all that fancy stuff all the other races use to cast spells, there is also a new kind of magic called Rune Magic. It uses resources instead of mana to cast powerful spells upon enemies. Dwarves also have their own set of units, like the vicious bear riders and rune priests.

The expansion adds new artifacts, new neutral monsters like mummies and manticores, new map objects and several new skirmish and multiplayer maps. It also reintroduces the Caravan feature, known from Heroes of Might and Magic IV, which allows you to transport units over the map without a hero in the group. In multiplayer matches, the simultaneous turn-based mode makes a return. It allows all players on a map to move their units without waiting for the other players during a turn.

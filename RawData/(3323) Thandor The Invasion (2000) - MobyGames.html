Description official descriptions

After the defeat of the Golraths, the Alliance of Averes have collapsed and the allies have turned enemies. But in the times of turmoil and despair, some faithful Alliance soldiers are planning for a return to the glorious Alliance...

Thandor is a 3D RTS game where you build units and battle other factions for control of resources (to build more units).

Trivia

Development for Thandor started in 1996; it was to be a 2D real-time strategy game. By fall 1997, developer Planet4 realized that their game was threatened to go unnoticed in a score of similar RTS titles that were flooding the market at that time. Despite the project's advanced status, it was re-designed from scratch with a 3D engine.

As a cruel irony of fate, Thandor did go quite unnoticed nevertheless.

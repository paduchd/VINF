Description official descriptions

Lula, star of Wet: The Sexy Empire, is the protagonist of this point-and-click adventure game. No longer the cutesy, comical girl of her youth, Lula has grown-up and has done very well for herself. Her movie career has taken off and she owns a lavish home in Beverly Hills, California. She is just beginning the production of her new film when tragedy strikes -- Three of her best porn stars are kidnapped! Using clues left behind, it is up to the player to guide the curvaceous Lula on their trail. Accompanied by her constant companion, Dusty the dog, travel across America and explore San Francisco, New Orleans' French Quarter and "sin city" Las Vegas.

The game has 3D graphics with free camera movement, and contains nudity and explicit sex scenes. The majority of the game is a traditional adventure in which the player has to talk to characters and use items to solve puzzles. A few action segments are included in the form of mini games and as part of the final conflict.

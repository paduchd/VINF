Description official description

Piposh 2 is the second game in the Piposh series. Like its predecessor, it is a humorous point and click adventure game.

In this game, following the first game's ending, you find yourself on an isolated island with an all new set of strange people, such as an old rich man who wants to build train rails all over the island, an inspector who worked as a tester for sucking candies and even a nerd who programs in Pascal. Your mission is to build a flying machine using the different items you can find in order to leave the island.

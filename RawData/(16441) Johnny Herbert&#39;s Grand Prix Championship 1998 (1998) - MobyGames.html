Description official descriptions

In Johnny Herbert's Grand Prix Championship 1998, the goal is to win the drivers championship and become the World Champion of 1998. To become the number one Formula 1 driver, the player will need to compete in 15 well-known Grand Prix circuits across the world. It's possible to race through each course in practice mode before going on to the qualification and later the actual race. The realism in the game can be adjusted for both sim and arcade fans.

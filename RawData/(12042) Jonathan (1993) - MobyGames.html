Trivia

Cancelled sequel

Phoenics announced a fourth adventure game, Sahara, the story of an expedition in search for a legendary treasure caravan lost in a mysterious region of the great desert. Sahara was never released.

Kronstadt

The fictional Kronstadt is modeled after the Bavarian town Memmingen.

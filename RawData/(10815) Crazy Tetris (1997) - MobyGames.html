Description

Crazy Tetris contains over a 100 Tetris games, both freeware and shareware. Each of them gives an unique twist to the classic game. Included are the original 1982 Tetris and more futuristic ones as well: SuperBlox, Boxes, BreakThru, Bricklayer, Brix... and many more.

Trivia

Legacy

One of the Tetris variants included here is better known as the "Xtet" mini-game from Gag: Het Impotentie-Mysterie, later also adapted into the mobile game Kamasutris.

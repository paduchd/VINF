Description official description

This special edition of Myst III: Exile includes:

  • the full game
  • a Myst III Strategy guide by Prima
  • soundtrack CD
  • a "Making of" CD that contains behind-the-scenes footage and trailers
  • a pewter figurine of one of the animals in the Myst world, the Squee (USA only)

Description

The 5th edition of the popular arcade dance music game comes home. Watch the arrows as they appear and step in the right direction at the right time. Difficulty ranges from beginner to insane. Diet mode offers you a fun way to exercise and lose weight. Edit mode allows you to create your own dance steps to your favorite songs, and there are plenty of extra songs and features to unlock.

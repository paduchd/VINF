Description

Xplosiv Top 10 is a compilation of 10 games of different genres, previously released separately on the Xplosiv budget label. Most games have patches and a digital manual included. The box comprises the following games:* Combat Chess

  • International Cricket Captain 2000
  • Panzer Dragoon
  • Pro Pinball: The Web
  • SEGA Touring Car Championship (as SEGA Touring Cars)
  • SEGA Worldwide Soccer
  • Sonic 3D
  • Sonic R
  • Virtua Cop 2
  • Virtua Fighter 2

Trivia

The cover lists Pro Pinball: Timeshock as one of the ten games, but the compilation actually includes the predecessor Pro Pinball: The Web.

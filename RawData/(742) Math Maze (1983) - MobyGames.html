Description official description

As a fly trapped in a maze, you run through the passageways, picking up the numbers that answer the math problems posed at the bottom of the screen. This game leads to some very fast action, especially with the spider, who will chase you down and kill you. This game allows for the players to design their own maze.

Trivia

Graphics

When you pass the current maze, a congratulation screen appears. In this screen, either the fly or the spider zips back and forth. While this happens, the background color quickly cycles through all 16 colors, but because the game puts a white background on the screen, it looks as if the black and magenta fly or the black and cyan spider is flashing very colorfully. This CGA trick is kind of like the blue background in the King's Quest series.

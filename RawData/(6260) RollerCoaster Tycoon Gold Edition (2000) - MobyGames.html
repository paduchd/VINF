Description official descriptions

RollerCoaster Tycoon: Gold Edition is a compilation of the theme-park management title RollerCoaster Tycoon with its expansion packs. Altogether, this package includes the following:

  • RollerCoaster Tycoon (base game)
  • RollerCoaster Tycoon: Corkscrew Follies (first expansion pack)
  • RollerCoaster Tycoon: Loopy Landscapes (second expansion pack)

Despite being called RollerCoaster Tycoon, the Xbox version also includes both add-ons. This is the first release on that platform.

Trivia

US version

The US version that was distributed on a single disc has a modified executable (version 1.20.015 as opposed to the latest Loopy Landscapes version 1.20.013) that is immune to common trainers, and includes certain fan-made track designs with garbled names. This is the release that was subsequently released on GOG and Steam, with select languages manually added later.

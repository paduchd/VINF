Description

Betasuppe is an unusual combination of text based commands and side-scrolling action.

Play as a strange voice inside the head of Colin, a well groomed, ever chipper, bluish thing. Colin will do whatever the player compels him to do, even die. The goal of the game is to help Colin survive another day, using all the items the player tells him to acquire on the way, while trying to find something that resembles a conclusion. The game utilizes many different keywords to make Colin move, some even as outlandish as starboardwards [sic].

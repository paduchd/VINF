Description official description

The second expansion pack to Heroes of Might and Magic IV adds six more singleplayer campaigns, 3 new creatures, and 45 scenarios.

Players will assume the roles of five conquerors, each staging his own uniquely driven invasion before facing a showdown for final control of the capitol city of Channon.

Description official description

The game that started it all is back for more, with even MORE deer to hunt! Get all the hunting accessories needed to outfit your virtual hunter, plus plenty of hunting guns and rifles. You can track, ambush or hunt through seven large wild landscapes, and even go online for a little friendly hunting competition. When you're really really bored, you can now play as the deer and evade the hunters! Featuring 3D acceleration support, the game also offers plenty of different modes (limit your armament choices, for example, to bows or muzzle loaders), as well as simulating different times of day, different seasons, and different weather conditions. Can you break the world record?

Description official descriptions

The Last Bounty Hunter is a simple shooter in the tradition of American Laser Games's Mad Dog McCree. Fairly simple in design, it features a video background with which you interact - you use your mouse or a light gun to literally shoot your foes and protect the innocent.

The story is this: you're a bounty hunter, one of the last, who come to a crime-infested Wild Western town to aid its Sheriff in getting rid of the criminals.

A completely horrible conversion.

The Good
Nothing. Nothing at all. I liked it on the arcade, but the PC version is horrible.

The Bad
The video playback is horrendously dissimilar to the original: crappy video compression techniques (even for a x1 CD-ROM) make the video look "cubed" instead of smooth, and in really low resolution too. The mouse controls are extremely uncomfortable and not faithful to the original arcade game, and I've seen better acting in B-movies. The lack of music or proper sound effects do not add much either.

The Bottom Line
A really bad conversion of a fairly typical, although quite fun, arcade game.

DOS · by Tomer Gabel (4539) · 2000

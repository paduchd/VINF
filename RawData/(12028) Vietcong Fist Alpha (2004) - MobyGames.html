Description official descriptions

Vietcong: Fist Alpha is the official expansion pack to Vietcong. This add-on requires the original game to play the seven new singleplayer and eight new multiplayer maps. The player is a member of Fist Alpha, a special forces unit of the US green berets and their job ranges from saving downed pilots to blowing up VC weapons facilities. The game also features a new array of weapons, a bayonet for close combat and planes that provide air support. A Campaign/Level-editor is included as well.

Same old thing.

The Good
Well obviously the new levels are pretty decent and the guns are quite good too at first.... Impressive multiplayer

The Bad
Well for a start, the new weapons don't add anything to the game at the end of the day,Its the basically the same levels as Vietcong except you play a different character.Same walking through endless jungles and having to follow brainless A.I soldiers at pace a sloth would be ashamed of.

The Bottom Line
It is a good game with good multiplayer and the new weapons and skins are nice until the novelty wears off.if your desperate for more Vietcong then I suppose it is worth but.If you want to be thrilled about something different rather than wandering through linear jungles the whole game then buy another game

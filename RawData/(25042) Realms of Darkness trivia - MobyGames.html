Trivia

Japanese title

When the game was ported to Japanese computers the name was changed to Rogue Alliance. According to former StarCraft employee Toshio Sato in a retrospective interview for RPG Codex: "the term Realms, from the title Realms of Darkness, wasn't something that would strike the Japanese people's eye no matter if we'd chosen to translate it into Japanese or just put it in katakana. As a consequence, I remember they decided to choose some words from the game's scenario for the title instead, which were supposed to leave a much deeper impression.â€‹"

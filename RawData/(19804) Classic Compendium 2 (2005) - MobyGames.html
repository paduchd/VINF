Description official description

The second collection of board games for the Gizmondo. This one has a more eastern approach than the first one. The games in this package are Taipei (aka Mahjong Solitaire), Chinese Checkers, Chinese Chess (aka Xiangqi), Shogi and Gomoku. The game has 14 different opponents and 10 practice levels. You can also get tips from the AI about how to play.

Trivia

Shogi

The program which the Shogi game is based on competed in the World Computer Shogi Championships. Although it ran on a low spec PC emulating the Gizmondo it still came 13th out of 53.

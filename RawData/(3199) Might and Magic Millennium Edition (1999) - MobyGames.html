Description

A partial compilation of the Might and Magic series. Game manuals and hint books are included as PDF files. The games included are:

  • Might and Magic: Clouds of Xeen
  • Might and Magic: Darkside of Xeen
  • Might and Magic: Swords of Xeen
  • Might and Magic VI: The Mandate of Heaven
  • Might and Magic VII: For Blood and Honor

The first two Xeen episodes combine into one large game, World of Xeen.

European release of the Millennium Edition contains only:

  • Might and Magic: Darkside of Xeen
  • Might and Magic VI: The Mandate of Heaven
  • Might and Magic VII: For Blood and Honor

Trivia

Extras

Although the manuals and strategy guides are in digital format on the six CDs of this compilation, it does come with two dual-sided mini poster maps depicting the full world of Xeen, Enroth, and Erathia.

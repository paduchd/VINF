Description official description

The second "Gold Games" compilation has a reduced number of games, compared to its predecessor (though, the number of CD's has increased).

The following games are included:

  • Action Soccer
  • Air Power
  • Apache
  • Bermuda Syndrome
  • C.E.O.
  • Chaos Control
  • Circle of Blood

Reviews

Critics

Players

Average score: 4.2 out of 5 (based on 14 ratings with 1 reviews)

Many (good) games for a low price.

The Good
Amazing game collection from 1997. A lot of games from all genres, my favourite ones were the adventures of this pack: Toonstruck, Kings Quest 7, Circle of Blood, Orion Burger, but also amazing strategy games like Z, Panzer General or Warwind.

MANY hours of gaming fun guaranteed.

The Bad
Not every game is a banger. But having 24 games is more than enough.

The Bottom Line
Gold Games 2 had effectively less games than Gold Games 1, but much more quality in it. GG 1 had some issues of starting in Windows PC having nearly DOS games, which GG 2 didnt have. GG 2 was a great succes, especially because of his low price of 50 Deutschmark when published.

Windows · by Phistra · 2023

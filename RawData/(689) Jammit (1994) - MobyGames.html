Description official descriptions

As the name implies, this is strictly a basketball game. It has a black-basketball theme (as seen in a lot of odd American movies) and has a myriad of game options (one on one, group, etc.). Also, it has a betting option, but other than that it's your typical run-of-the-mill basketball game.

One of the most horrible games I have ever played.

The Good
Yuck! Thwarp! Blat! Good in this game? Hardly.

The Bad
A truly annoying game with lamebrain photographed graphics and horribly underdeveloped game engine. Stupid AI to the point of having the computer just stand put when you 'break the net' doesn't add much to the game either. Lack of music and horrendous sound effects complete the all-around stupidity of this so-called computer entertainment title.

The Bottom Line
A horrible game you should never, ever play.

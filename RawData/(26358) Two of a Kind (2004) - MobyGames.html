Description

The town of Bluff City was founded in the area where an asteroid came crashing down five hundred years earlier. After a while, the pioneering citizens noticed something strange: the next generations were born with special abilities such as invisibility, flight or transformation. In this freeware adventure, the player controls private investigators and fraternal twins Tim and Tiffany Walters in Bluff City. This game deals with a case where the duo needs to retrieve a stolen crystal.

Using a classic click-and-point interface, both Tim and Tiffany can be guided. The bottom right corner holds their portraits where they can be selected individually. Tim has the ability to float and Tiffany talks with animals, two important powers needed to solve the puzzles. Visiting locations such as a mansion, a cave, a store, a farm and the home office, the two need to combine items, examine the environment and talk to other characters to discover the crystal's location, which ultimately leads to a more sinister plot.

Trivia

The game was created for ATC ("AGS Team Competition"), a contest held twice a year on the AGS (Adventure Game Studio) forums. The idea behind the contest is to get people of different skill sets to work on a game and finish within a designated amount of time. The entire game was created in 6 weeks.

The plot was inspired by Orson Scott Card's book Seventh Son, where people have "knacks", special powers.

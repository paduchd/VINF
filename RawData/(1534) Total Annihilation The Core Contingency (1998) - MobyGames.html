Description

Total Annihilation: The Core Contingency is an add-on to the RTS game Total Annihilation. It adds new units, maps, campaigns and a mission/map editor.

Amongst the new units are seaplanes, hovercraft, and the Krogoth K-Bot, a humongous mech that is 4 times the size of the next biggest land unit and can single-handedly lay waste to an entire enemy base.

The Good
The game had lots of units and maps already but now there is even more then before. The new units brought the game a new technologie and so there had to be new maps. The best part is that cavedog has new units and maps that you can download from cavedog's official site.

The Bad
Total Annihilation needed a better story line or something big that could have made the story a little more interresting but if you really like explosions, tons of units and huge guns you're gonna love this game.

The Bottom Line
This game is a must if total annihilation is already in your library. If not then you gotta give it a try.

I give it 4.85 out of 5

Description official description

RedJack: Revenge of the Brethren is a pre-rendered adventure game featuring 3D action sequences.

The game follows the story of Nicholas Dove, a boy with a thirst for adventure and the high seas. What starts as a quest for simple excitement could very well end up with him walking the plank in a sea full of sharks, or being slashed apart by a sword, as Nicholas senses that his fate is pre-ordained, and tied somehow with the mighty pirate RedJack, and the vow of vengeance he made 17 years ago.

Trivia

RedJack: The Revenge of the Brethren was originally intended as a pirate musical. A number of songs were recorded for it before it was "laughed out of a focus group", causing its shift to the darker game it became.

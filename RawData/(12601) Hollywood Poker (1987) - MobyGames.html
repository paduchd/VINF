Description official descriptions

This strip poker game features 4 women (including a former Miss Germany), each consisting of 5 scanned photos. The game is accompanied by an intro and outro tune.

Trivia

Development

The original C64 version was made within two weeks. Holger Gehrmann finished the complete coding within a few days, Dieter Eckhardt hired a photographer and four models. One of the models became Miss Germany later, then Miss Europe and as of 2007 she works for a German TV station as a host.

Description official description

You will be driving a jeep through a maze (top-down view). Of course such mazes, as we all know, are often not very safe when they are in a video game. Tanks and helicopters will appear on the screen and you will have to shoot them before they shoot you. If you are hit by enemy fire you will lose a life.

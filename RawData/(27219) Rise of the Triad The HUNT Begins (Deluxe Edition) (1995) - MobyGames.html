Description

This deluxe edition includes the ten single-player and eight multi-player levels of the original shareware version of Rise of the Triad, as well as six new levels (three single-player and three multiplayer ones) exclusive to this release.

Unlike the regular shareware version of the game, the Deluxe Edition was only available on CDs through the publisher, Lasersoft.

Trivia

Add-on release for the full game

In October 1999, when the Deluxe Edition was discontinued, Apogee released the six extra levels as a free download which is available from the official Apogee/3D Realms website. These levels can be used both with the registered version and the shareware version of Rise of the Triad.

Authorship of the extra levels

Even though the exclusive Deluxe Edition levels are officially referred to as the Lasersoft Shareware Levels, they were all made by Tom Hall.

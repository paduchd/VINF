Description

This edition was limited to 4,000 copies and could only be purchased through Ubisoft in the UK. It contains:

  • Heroes of Might and Magic V (Deluxe Edition)
  • A numbered and authenticated case adorned with the likenesses of heroes Godric and Agrael.
  • A coin purse with a Heroes V coin (one side showing the emblem of the Holy Griffin Empire and the other the symbol of Urgash, the dragon of Chaos). One of the 4,000 editions has a special coin. Instead of a regular solver coin, it is a Golden Thaler, a relic from ruler Ivan Griffon that is said to provide the bearer with absolute power.

Trivia

Competition

The player who had the luck of buying the copy with the Golden Thaler, was the winner of a competition organized by Ubisoft - The Search for the Golden Thaler - and won an entire computer.

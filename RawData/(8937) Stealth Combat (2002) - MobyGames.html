Description official descriptions

In 2038, a war rages between the EMA ( Economic and Military Alliance) and the Great Empire. You must choose what side you want to be on, as you are going to undertake 25 infiltration, reconnaissance, and wipe out missions behind the wheel of the most powerful military hardware that the Special Forces possess. Tanks, helicopters, jeeps, amphibious vehicles, etc. Nothing is over the top. However, be careful: whatever situation you are faced with during your missions, never forget which side you are defending.

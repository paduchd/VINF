Description

Logical Journey of the Zoombinis is an educational game designed to promote logical thinking in children of all ages. When the Zoombinis home, Zoombini Isle, is taken over by creatures called Bloats, they decide to escape to a new home. But they can only 16 at a time, and they must cross many obstacles that require logic to get past. It's up to you to get past all the puzzles to get all of the Zoombinis to their new home.

The Good
The game has a very user-friendly interface and employs fantastic use of colour, animation and other multimedia to attract users immediately. The exciting games and activities that the user can participate in make the product very appealing to a majority of audiences.

The Bad
The game is outdated and does not possess the features commonly employed in computer software today. Other than that, the product itself has very limited negativity.

The Bottom Line
A fun-filled, exciting adventure game that combines education and enjoyment to create a very entertaining piece of software.

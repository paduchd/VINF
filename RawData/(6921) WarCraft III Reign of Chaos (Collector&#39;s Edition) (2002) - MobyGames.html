Description official description

This collector's edition includes:

  • WarCraft III: Reign of Chaos
  • a bonus DVD with cinematics, interviews and a making-of segment
  • an Art of Warcraft coffee table book
  • 4 limited-edition lithographs
  • a signed manual
  • soundtrack disc

Trivia

For those who ordered this game from EBWorld.com (now EBGames.com), same as with standard version of the game, they received an extra DVD with trailers for this game and World of WarCraft. However, for those who ordered this Collector's Edition over standard edition, they also got an extra poster of a human knight charging the attack on the hordes from the first in-game cinematic.

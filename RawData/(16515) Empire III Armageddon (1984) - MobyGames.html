Description

The final episode of the Empire Science Fiction Adventure trilogy reveals a world in chaos. In this desperate society you have but one mission: to find the Empress and destroy the Empire. But first, you must survive the City, defy the lethal snares of the ghetto, and escape the Arena's bloody sport. You'll meet vandals, racketeers, prostitutes, beggars, preachers and Imperial guards. With each encounter, you'll stake your life in a gamble against fate. And if you escape the City, new treachery awaits in the brooding Wilderness. Your cunning may see you through, but what perils lie within the Great Pyramid?

Trivia

Educational label

While Edu-Ware Services was developing this game, Management Sciences of America bought the company and determined that it should solely focus on creating educational software to be published by MSA's Peachtree Software subsidiary. As a result, the game's packaging portrayed it as an "Educational" product, and it was the only game in the Empire saga to be published under the Peachtree Software label instead of the Edu-Ware label.

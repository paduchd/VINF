Description

In this game your job is to move a tower (big pieces at the bottom, small pieces at the top) from the left to the right using a middle pad.

The problem is that per turn you can only move the highest piece of the tower, and only so that the current piece you move can only be placed upon a bigger piece. This means you have to think ahead how you will swap the towers.

Depending on the level you select, you will have a starting tower with 3 pieces or up to 12 pieces.

Trivia

Turns

The optimal number of turns can be calculated using the easy formula of (2^n)-1, where n is the number of pieces.

If we assume that a player who does not need to think, needs about 2 seconds for every piece and all moves are perfect (no errors), this player can beat level 1 (3 pieces) in 14 seconds. However, this person would need 2 hours and 16 minutes for level 10 (12 pieces).

Description

In a futuristic world, where magicians and legendary creatures co-exist with robots and clones, the peaceful existence of Far the Earth is threatened by the Emperor of Evil. Atima, a young girl from the magician guild, is entrusted with a highly important task: reviving Dimeora, the legendary hero from the epoch of gods, who alone is capable of defeating the Emperor of Evil. But things take an unexpected turn as Atima is kidnapped by the Emperor, and the revived Dimeora has to find his way alone in this new world.

Neo Metal Fantasy is a traditional Japanese-style RPG. Traveling over the world map, the player-controlled party visits towns, descend into dungeons, and engage randomly appearing enemies in first-person perspective, turn-based combat, using physical attacks or magic. The game features anime-style cutscenes.

Trivia

Easter Eggs are unlocked on certain days of the year.

January 1st Fireworks in the courtyard.

January 9th Hearts on Snack pickups, Daisies on all other pickups.

February 2nd Daisies on Snack pickups.

February 14th Hearts on Snack pickups.

March 17th Fountain is green. Chimneys on emit green smoke.

March 22nd Fireworks in the courtyard.

May 30th Fireworks in the courtyard.

July 4th Fountain and chimneys on house are colored red, white & blue. Fireworks in the courtyard are also red, white & blue.

August 28th Fireworks in the courtyard. Fountains are multicolored.

September 8th Fireworks in the courtyard.

October 31st Bats hanging in the courtyard.

November 5th Fireworks in the courtyard.

December 14th Snows in the courtyard.

December 25th Snows in the courtyard.

December 31st Fireworks in the courtyard.

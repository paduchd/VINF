Description official description

The official PBA Bowling game lets you create your own custom bowler, and play single games, team games, or even tournament! Play online against other PC or even Sega Dreamcast players! You have complete control over the ball. Every aspect of ball physics is simulated, down to the wear of the bowling lanes. Multiple camera angles let you savor every moment of your triumph.

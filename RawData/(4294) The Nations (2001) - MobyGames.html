Description official descriptions

The sequel to Alien Nations , the Amazons, the Pimmons, and the Sajikis are back with more options than ever. Trade, conduct diplomacy, impression other tribes in your nation, or conquer by default instead of by war, it's all up to you!

The Nations is a real-time strategy game with emphasis on resource management instead of combat.

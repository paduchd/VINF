Description official descriptions

Wet Attack: The Empire Cums Back is a follow-up to Lula: The Sexy Empire. The main character searches for Lula, who is lost in space. On board the spaceship "The Tit" the protagonist must explore faraway planets and gather information concerning Lula's whereabouts.

The game is primarily a managerial simulation with light space trading and combat elements. The player navigates the ship between planets, transporting cargo for money. Hiring a crew is also essential; since crew members are all female, the player character must either sexually satisfy them by himself, or hire gigolos to do the job. The ship can be upgraded: engines, guns, shields, spy satellites can be purchased or found. While docking at a planet, the ship turns into a brothel, which the player has to manage in order to keep virtual customers happy.

Description

A combination of a slot machine and bingo. You are presented with a board of numbers. Press the spin button to spin the numbers at the bottom. If a number you need comes up, vertically, below one of your rows, press it to clear it. If a joker comes up, you can select any number in that vertical row. If a coin comes up, you get the points. If you spin a devil, you will lose half your points unless a cherub randomly saves you by shooting the devil. After sixteen spins, each spin will cost you points. If you spun a free spin, you can use it with no point loss. Spin seventeen will cost you five hundred points, spin eighteen, one thousand and so on to the last, twentieth, spin. If you clear the entire board by or before twenty spins, you receive a bonus.

If this were in Las Vegas, there would be a quarter shortage...

The Good
I love this game. It's mindless fun, has the addictive quality of Tetris, and is just plain cool.

The Gameplay is very simple. You start out with a Bingo Card with random numbers. You have 20 spins to completely cover the card for a "Slingo". Each pull of the lever brings up 5 numbers on the bottom of the screen. Match the numbers that are spun to the numbers on the card to win! Simple, yet fun.

5 Different versions are included on the CD. Classic, Duel, Giant, Super Squares and Mixed Matrix. Each has it's own varitaions, and they are all fun.

Power ups are present in the game also. If a Joker appears when you spin, it's a wild card... You can cover any available space in the row it appears in. The Devil will take half of your money away (unless the angel shows up, then you're saved), and you also get Free Spins and Point bonuses.

After 16 spins, you start getting points taken away. That's where the Free Spins come in. They allow you to play without the loss of points.

The Bad
Actually, I think it's a great game. Not much to complain about here!

The Bottom Line
If you've played it online (over AOL), then you'll love the home version! (Sounds like The old Wheel Of Fortune ads... Try our great Home Game!)

If you're looking for a mindless game that's really addicting. Pick it up... Great Game

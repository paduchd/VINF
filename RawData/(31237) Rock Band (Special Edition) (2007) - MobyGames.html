Description

This Special Edition includes:

  • Rock Band
  • Fender Stratocaster guitar controller (PS2 and PS3: wireless, Xbox 360: wired)
  • Electronic drum kit with drum sticks
  • Microphone
  • Additional software
  • USB hub (Xbox 360/PS2/Wii)

Description official description

Time of Defiance is a massively multiplayer online real time strategy game. The game takes place on Nespanona, a shattered planet consisting of thousands of islands. Each player has its own empire with control of one island. They then have to explore and conquer as large part of the game world as possible. As in other strategy games, resources must be gathered, to build vehicles that are used to defend the home island and scout the world. Diplomacy can also be used when the player don't want to use his guns to solve a conflict.

The big difference from other strategy games is the scope of the game. Each game can go on for a month, and be played by hundreds of players. Therefore the game will go on even when you're away from the computer. For that reason it's important to build defenses that protects your empire while you're away. If that happens the game will send you an e-mail to inform you.

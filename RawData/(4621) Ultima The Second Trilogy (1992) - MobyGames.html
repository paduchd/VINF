Description official description

This is the second Special Edition compendium of Ultima games, containing the second 'trilogy' of the Ultima series (also known as the Age of Enlightenment):

  • Ultima IV: Quest of the Avatar
  • Ultima V: Warriors of Destiny
  • Ultima VI: The False Prophet

Trivia

The first DOS release of the 2nd Trilogy included a paper map, Age of Virtue Compendium and a 24 page Player reference card. The original cost was $79.95 - a high price even for today's standards.

A "Special Limited Edition: was released later with more goodies - cloth maps instead of paper and a trinket.

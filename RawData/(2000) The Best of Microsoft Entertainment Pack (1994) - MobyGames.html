Description official description

The Best of Microsoft Entertainment Pack is a collection of 13 puzzle games that include:

  • Tetris,
  • Freecell,
  • Pipe Dream,
  • Chip's Challenge,
  • Taipei,
  • Tut's Tomb,
  • Rodent's Revenge,
  • TriPeaks,
  • Golf,
  • Ski Free,
  • JezzBall,
  • Dr. Black Jack,
  • and TetraVex.

Trivia

Release

All these games were previously released in the Microsoft Entertainment Packs 1, 2, 3 and 4.

Sample discs

In 1993, floppy disk manufacturer Verbatim Corporation bundled a free Microsoft Entertainment Pack Sampler with a selection from all four original volumes and complete games on a bonus eleventh disk in specially marked boxes of DataLife 3.5" High Density (HD) 10 Floppy Disks.

Volume 1 featured * Taipei * FreeCell * SkiFree * Dr. Black Jack

Volume 2 featured * Cruel * Jezzball * Rodent's Revenge * Klotski

Description official descriptions

Race as fast as you can and win money! A former policeman won the lottery and decided to hold races to give away some of the winnings... Featuring seven different American tracks (each with surprises!), and nine different vehicles, with more vehicles available from the official website. All the vehicles are upgradable and features visible damage that affects the car's handling. Use Nitro to get a quick boost, and stay clear of the cops!

A racing game for a cheap, arcade style thrill

The Good
Fun, arcade style racing game, great tracks, great layout, and I really enjoy the obstacles setup on each track. Graphics are quite nice and even to this day they still look good.

The Bad
Music isn't very good, sound effects are mediocre, controls have some flaws, especially with steering. If you are using a controller it would probably the be worst to use. Some of the tracks are a bit too long for a racing game as well.

The Bottom Line
Fun, action packed, arcade style racing game, with 7 tracks throughout America, and Mexico, you get to choose between 9 different cars, and 3 different game modes (Arcade, Championship, and Multiplayer). Each race you are up against 5 opponents and you try to bump, bang, and speed your way to the top spot.

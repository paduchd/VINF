Description

Command 40 actors (Live Actors filmed/photographed and put into the game) on over 100 sets with more than 300 props to create your own scenes. Lighting can also be changed to fit a certain scene along with music and sound effects. Adapt literature or write you own material and put it in the actors mouths. Although the game leans toward a classic British mystery the many combinations can give you almost limitless themes.

This game was developed with the cooperation of the The Children's Theatre Company of Minneapolis who are most of the in game actors.

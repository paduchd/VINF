Description

Star Chamber is an online multiplayer strategy game. Players play one of the nine races that are part of the Galactic Republic. Maneuvering through the galaxy with ships players try to outsmart each other with their deck of virtual cards. During the game, the different races try to use their racial advantage to outmatch the opponent and achieve dominance on one of the three fields: Military, Culture or Politics. These are three different victory types where each lends itself to a wide range of different playing styles.

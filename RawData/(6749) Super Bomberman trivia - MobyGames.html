Trivia

Super MultiTap 2

In Japan, HudsonSoft released the Super MultiTap 2, which is designed to look just a like a Bomberman head.

Awards

  • Electronic Gaming Monthly
    • October 1993 (issue 51) - Game of the Month* Game Informer Magazine
    • August 2001 (Issue 100) - voted #90 in the Top 100 Games of All Time poll* GamePro
    • February 1994 (Vol. 6, Issue 2) - named Strategy/Puzzle Game of the Year in the 1993 Editors' Choice Awards

Information also contributed by Big John WV and PCGamer77.

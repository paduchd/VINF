Description official description

NASCAR Legends takes all the top drivers, cars, and most of the tracks from the 1970 NASCAR season. It used a modified version of the NASCAR 2 engine, but all 4 different cars (Ford Torino, Plymouth Superbird, Dodge Daytona, and Mercury) each have a different look and feel. The tracks are detailed from 1970.

It also added force feedback support.

Description official descriptions

This is a compilation of Medal of Honor: Allied Assault and Medal of Honor: Allied Assault - Spearhead which also includes strategy guides for every single and multiplayer level. A bonus CD with a behind-the-scenes look at development of the next Medal of Honor title. Also on the bonus CD: new WWII based character skins.

Trivia

Soundtrack

The fourth deluxe edition disc also contains a soundtrack from Medal of Honor Allied Assault, composed by Michael Giacchino. The disc cannot be played on hi-fi as the tracks are in mp3 format.

The tracks included are: 1. Attack on Fort Schmerzen 2. Colditz Castle 3. Fleeing the Catacombs 4. Jaques Death 5. Labyrinth of the Minotaur 6. Main Theme 7. Nordhausen 8. North Africa 9. Panzer Attack 10. Panzer Blockade 11. Schmerzen 12. September 1st, 1939 13. Sniper Town 14. Taking Out the Railgun 15. The Jet Aircraft Facility 16. The U-Boat 17. Tiger Tank

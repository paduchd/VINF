Description

Protivostoyanie: Opaleonniy sneg is an add-on for the real-time strategy game Counter Action, devoted to the winter period of World War II. It brings 14 full-scaled single missions, 15 maps for network play, 6 types of units, winter landscapes, new cut-scenes, buildings and objects, a multiplayer mode and an extensive manual.

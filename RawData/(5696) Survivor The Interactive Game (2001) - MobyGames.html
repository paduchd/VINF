Description official descriptions

Survivor: The Interactive Game is a game version of the TV show. Players choose one of the contestants, who are all real contestants from Survivor: The Australian Outback or they can create their own player and distribute stat points, in categories such as Cooking, Hunting, Fishing, Strength, etc.

Once in the game, players must play through a survival period, in which you will carry out your allotted task. If you chose to cook, your player will spend his/her time around the fire, cooking if you have any available meat. After the survival period, you will move on to the challenge round, where you take part in challenges taken directly from the show. After the challenge round, you move on to Tribal Council, where one of you will be voted off (unless you won immunity in the challenge round).

Survivor gives you the opportunity to play a full game (13 episodes), a short game, which takes place after the two tribes merge into one or a quick game, with three people remaining.

Description

The 2007 version of the series improves over the 2005 game with a new 2D game simulation and expanded tactical options to coach and review the games. Many of the decisions can now be set using sliders. For the 2007 edition, the game database boasts some 45,000 players and staff, and 3,000 teams from the world of hockey. The general look and feel of the game has also been revamped.

You can also build your own dream team using fantasy draft. Other enhancements include extra trade options and feedback, greater salary cap control and more information on individual cap hits.

This version was released via electronic purchase only - eLicense on PC, and eSellerate on Macintosh.
